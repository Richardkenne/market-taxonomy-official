Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the Market Taxonomy framework and how market categories are defined.

Why do most markets have 5–6 Level 1 categories?

The number of Level 1 categories is not predefined. Across most macro markets, convergent validation by institutional authorities, market intelligence, operators, and capital allocation actors typically results in a limited set of stable categories. This recurring pattern reflects structural clarity and institutional practice, not an imposed numerical constraint.
