Payments & Transaction Processing

Card networks, payment gateways, merchant acquiring, and real-time payment rails enabling the movement of money between parties — processing over $2 quadrillion annually in global transaction volume.

Segments follow BIS Committee on Payments and Market Infrastructures (CPMI) statistics, McKinsey Global Payments Report classifications, and World Bank Global Payment Systems Survey categories.

Global payments revenue reached $2.4 trillion in 2024 (McKinsey), growing 7% YoY. Real-time payments are live in 70+ countries. The shift from cash to digital payments adds ~$500B in new payment flows annually, with mobile money leading financial inclusion in Sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia.

How to Choose a Niche

Payment orchestration and cross-border treasury management are the strongest SaaS niches — merchants need multi-PSP routing and FX optimization. B2B payments remain largely manual ($125T annually, mostly via wire/check) — automating AP/AR workflows for mid-market companies is a massive opportunity. Avoid building another consumer wallet — that market is winner-take-all.
