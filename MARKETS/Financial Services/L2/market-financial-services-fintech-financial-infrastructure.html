How to Choose a Niche

RegTech is the safest entry point — compliance is mandatory spend, not discretionary. Target specific regulatory domains (AML transaction monitoring for crypto exchanges, DORA compliance for EU financial institutions, or ESG reporting for asset managers). BaaS requires banking partnerships and regulatory licensing — higher barrier but stronger moat. Open banking middleware for specific verticals (accounting, payroll, lending) offers the best risk/reward.
