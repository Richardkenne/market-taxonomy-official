Insurance

Life, health, property & casualty, and specialty insurance underwriting and distribution — driven by actuarial modeling, regulatory capital requirements, and claims management across $6.8 trillion in global premiums.

Segments follow Swiss Re sigma classifications, IAIS insurance statistics, Deloitte Insurance Outlook categories, and McKinsey Global Insurance Report segmentation aligned with GICS insurance sub-industries.

Sources

Global insurance premiums reached $6.8 trillion in 2024 (Swiss Re). The protection gap — uninsured risk — exceeds $1.8 trillion annually, concentrated in emerging markets. InsurTech investment has reshaped distribution but underwriting and claims remain the primary cost centers ripe for automation.

How to Choose a Niche

Claims automation and underwriting AI are the highest-value software niches. Target mid-market carriers (premium volume $500M-$5B) — they lack the tech budgets of top-10 carriers but face the same regulatory and efficiency pressures. Embedded insurance APIs offer a distribution-side opportunity if you can partner with platforms that already own the customer relationship.
