How to Choose a Niche

Fund administration and investor reporting automation for mid-market PE/VC firms ($1-10B AUM) is the strongest niche. Portfolio monitoring platforms that standardize financial data collection from portfolio companies also have strong demand. Secondaries marketplace platforms connecting LP sellers with buyers are an emerging opportunity.
