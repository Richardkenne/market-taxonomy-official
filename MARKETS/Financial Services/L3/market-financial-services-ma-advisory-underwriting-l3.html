How to Choose a Niche

Virtual data room platforms and AI-powered due diligence tools are the most accessible niches. Target middle-market investment banks and PE firms — they handle 10-50 deals annually and need workflow tools that bulge-bracket banks build internally. Financial modeling automation for analysts is a strong adjacent play.
