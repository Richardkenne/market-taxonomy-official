How to Choose a Niche

Automated underwriting for simplified-issue life products and health insurance claims AI are the strongest niches. Target mid-market carriers — top-10 carriers build in-house, but carriers ranked 20-100 need vendor solutions. Group benefits administration for mid-market employers (100-5000 employees) is also underserved.
