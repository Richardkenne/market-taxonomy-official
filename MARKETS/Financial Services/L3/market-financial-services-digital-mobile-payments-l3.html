How to Choose a Niche

Real-time payment infrastructure and fraud prevention for instant payments are the strongest niches. Target payment service providers (PSPs) and neobanks that need to support multiple payment rails across geographies. Unified payment acceptance platforms for SMEs in emerging markets also have strong product-market fit.
