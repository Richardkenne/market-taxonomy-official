How to Choose a Niche

Advisor productivity platforms that consolidate CRM, portfolio management, compliance, and client reporting are the strongest niche. Target independent RIAs and multi-family offices — they manage $500M-$5B AUM with small teams and need integrated workflows. Client portal and reporting tools for UHNW families are a premium adjacent play.
