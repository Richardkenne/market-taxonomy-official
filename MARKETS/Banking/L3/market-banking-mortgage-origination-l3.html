How to Choose a Niche

Digital point-of-sale platforms have the largest TAM as every lender needs a borrower-facing application portal. Automated underwriting is the fastest-growing niche with AI-driven income and asset verification cutting days from timelines. eClosing and RON are growing but adoption is state-dependent. Focus on automated underwriting — lenders who cut origination time from 45 to 20 days win market share.
