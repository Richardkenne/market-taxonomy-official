Commercial & Business Banking

Lending, treasury management, trade finance, and deposit services for SMEs, mid-market companies, and large corporates — with relationship-driven pricing and Basel III/IV capital requirements.

Segments follow Federal Reserve Y-14 reporting categories, OCC commercial lending classifications, and BIS Basel Committee corporate banking exposure classes.

Sources

Fed Commercial Lending Data OCC Supervision BIS Basel Committee McKinsey Banking

Global commercial banking generates over $1.8 trillion in annual revenues (McKinsey). Corporate lending remains the anchor product, but treasury and transaction banking now contribute 35-40% of revenues with higher ROE. SME banking is the fastest-digitizing segment, with embedded lending and automated credit decisioning reshaping origination.

How to Choose a Niche

SME lending automation and treasury management dashboards offer the strongest SaaS opportunities. Target community banks and regional banks lacking modern commercial loan origination systems. Supply chain finance platforms and automated trade finance document processing are growing rapidly with strong unit economics.
