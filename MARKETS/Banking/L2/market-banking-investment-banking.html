Investment Banking

Advisory services, capital markets underwriting, and securities trading for institutional clients — spanning M&A, IPOs, debt issuance, restructuring, and structured finance.

Segments follow SEC reporting categories, Dealogic league table classifications, and GICS Diversified Financial Services sub-industry definitions for investment banking and brokerage.

Sources

SEC EDGAR Filings S&P Capital IQ BIS Securities Statistics McKinsey Capital Markets

Global investment banking fee pools reached $75-85 billion annually, with the top 10 banks capturing 55%+ market share. M&A advisory and ECM underwriting are highly cyclical; DCM is more stable. Technology is reshaping deal execution — AI-powered due diligence, virtual data rooms, and algorithmic pricing are creating software opportunities across the value chain.

How to Choose a Niche

AI-powered deal sourcing, virtual data room platforms, and automated due diligence tools are the most accessible software opportunities. Target mid-market investment banks and boutique advisory firms. Avoid competing with Bloomberg/Refinitiv on market data — instead focus on workflow automation, compliance document generation, and client reporting.
