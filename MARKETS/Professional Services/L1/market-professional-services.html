29 Professional Services

Professional services markets are segmented by discipline, client type, and delivery model — each with distinct talent economics, billing structures, and knowledge-management requirements.

We reference two types of sources: industry classification standards used by statistical agencies to categorize knowledge-work firms, and consulting industry research used to size service lines and track demand trends. Use these to validate segment definitions before narrowing to a specific practice area or firm size.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on segments with project-based billing complexity and utilization-rate pressure. Examples: professional services automation (PSA) software for IT consulting firms managing multi-project portfolios; time-and-billing platforms for mid-size accounting practices; proposal automation tools for engineering firms responding to RFPs; knowledge-management systems for management consulting teams.
