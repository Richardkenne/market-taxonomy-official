Spherical Graphite Processing

Spheroidization, purification, and coating technology for converting natural flake graphite into battery-grade coated spherical graphite (CSPG) for lithium-ion anodes.

Silicon-Graphite Composite Anodes

Silicon nanoparticle integration into graphite anode matrices for next-generation lithium-ion batteries with 20-40% higher energy density.

Vanadium Redox Flow Batteries

Vanadium electrolyte production, membrane technology, and system integration for long-duration grid-scale energy storage applications.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on supply chain localization — DLE lithium, rare earth separation, and graphite anode processing outside China are the highest-priority segments for Western government subsidies (IRA, EU CRM Act). Responsible cobalt sourcing serves a regulatory mandate. Silicon-graphite anodes represent the next battery material transition.
