Responsible Cobalt Sourcing

Digital traceability, mine-site monitoring, and third-party audit systems for responsible cobalt supply chains meeting OECD due diligence and EU Battery Regulation requirements.

Cobalt Hydroxide Processing

Selective cobalt precipitation and purification from copper-cobalt leach solutions, producing battery-grade cobalt hydroxide and sulphate.

Cobalt-Free Battery R&D

Research platforms and material testing services for validating cobalt-free cathode chemistries (LFP, LMFP, sodium-ion) at commercial scale.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on supply chain localization — DLE lithium, rare earth separation, and graphite anode processing outside China are the highest-priority segments for Western government subsidies (IRA, EU CRM Act). Responsible cobalt sourcing serves a regulatory mandate. Silicon-graphite anodes represent the next battery material transition.
