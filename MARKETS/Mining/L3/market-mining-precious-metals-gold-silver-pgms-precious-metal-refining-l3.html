How to Choose a Niche

Focus on the gold-to-green transition: PGM recycling from spent autocatalysts, hydrogen fuel cell catalyst optimization, or ESG-compliant precious metal traceability. Mid-tier gold miners ($500M-$5B market cap) are actively seeking technology to address refractory ore challenges and declining grades.
