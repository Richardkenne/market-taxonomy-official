How to Choose a Niche

Focus on regulatory-driven demand — tailings dam monitoring (GISTM), mine carbon accounting (CSRD/ISSB), and collision avoidance systems have mandatory adoption curves. AI-augmented geological modeling and autonomous equipment serve the industry's productivity imperative. Contract mining analytics address the fastest-growing operational model.
