How to Choose a Niche

Cloud POS for specific verticals (restaurants, grocery, specialty retail) is a proven model — Toast proved category-specific POS wins over horizontal players. Self-checkout loss prevention technology is a growing niche as retailers grapple with theft. Unified commerce middleware connecting legacy POS to modern e-commerce platforms has strong demand from mid-market retailers not ready for full replacement.
