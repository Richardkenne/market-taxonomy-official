How to Choose a Niche

Distributed order management for mid-market retailers (too complex for Shopify, too small for Manhattan Associates) has strong whitespace. RFID-based inventory accuracy solutions are growing rapidly as tag costs decrease below $0.03. Ship-from-store optimization — store picking productivity, routing, and cost allocation — is an emerging niche as retailers expand store fulfillment. Avoid building general WMS — focus on retail-specific workflows.
