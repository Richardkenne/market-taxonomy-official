How to Choose a Niche

Size and fit technology (3D body scanning, AI size recommendation) directly addresses the $100B+ returns problem and has strong PMF signals. Apparel PLM (product lifecycle management) tools for faster design-to-shelf cycles have proven demand. Sustainability and supply chain traceability platforms for apparel are riding regulatory tailwinds (EU textile regulations). Resale/recommerce infrastructure (ThredUp model) is growing fast.
