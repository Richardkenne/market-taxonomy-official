How to Choose a Niche

Headless commerce components (search, checkout, PDP) are a growing niche — build one piece exceptionally well rather than a full platform. Commerce middleware and integration platforms (connecting composable tools) have strong demand. International commerce enablement (multi-currency, cross-border tax, localized checkout) is an underserved horizontal need. Avoid building another Shopify competitor — focus on enterprise/mid-market or specific verticals.
