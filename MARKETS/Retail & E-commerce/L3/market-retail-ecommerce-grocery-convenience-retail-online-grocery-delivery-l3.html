Micro-fulfillment automation and dark store operations software have strong VC interest and retailer demand. Delivery route optimization specifically for grocery (temperature-sensitive, time-window constrained) is differentiated from general logistics. Substitution AI and real-time inventory accuracy for online grocery picking are unsolved problems with clear value.
