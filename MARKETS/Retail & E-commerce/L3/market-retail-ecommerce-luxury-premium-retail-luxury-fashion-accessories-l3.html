How to Choose a Niche

Luxury clienteling platforms replacing spreadsheets and basic CRM are a proven category with high willingness to pay ($50-100K+ per boutique). Authentication technology using AI, NFC, and blockchain serves both brands and resale platforms. Digital Product Passport compliance tools address an imminent EU regulatory requirement. Luxury e-commerce personalization (not generic Shopify) has whitespace.
