AI-Native CRM

Next-generation CRM platforms that auto-capture activities, auto-enrich contacts, and use AI to surface deal insights — replacing manual data entry with ambient intelligence.

Vertical Sales CRM

Industry-specific CRM solutions with built-in workflows for real estate, financial services, healthcare sales, and other verticals with unique selling motions.

Sales Engagement & Outreach

Multi-channel sales engagement platforms automating email sequences, call cadences, social touches, and meeting scheduling for SDR and AE teams.

How to Choose a Niche

AI-native CRM and vertical sales CRM are the highest-opportunity niches. Target teams of 5-50 reps in industries with complex or non-standard sales motions.
