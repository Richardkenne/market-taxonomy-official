Enterprise AI Search & Knowledge

AI-powered platforms that index enterprise data (docs, Slack, email, wikis) and provide natural language search and question-answering — making organizational knowledge instantly accessible.

Vertical AI Copilots

Domain-specific AI assistants built for particular industries or functions — legal document drafting, medical note-taking, financial analysis, and compliance review.

AI Agent Frameworks & Orchestration

Platforms and frameworks for building, deploying, and managing autonomous AI agents that can perform multi-step tasks, use tools, and collaborate with humans in the loop.

How to Choose a Niche

Vertical AI copilots and enterprise AI search have the strongest PMF signals right now. AI agent orchestration is high-potential but very early. Avoid building general-purpose AI assistants — compete on domain depth rather than model quality.
