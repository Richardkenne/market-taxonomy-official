CI/CD & DevOps

The automation backbone of software delivery — CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure-as-code, container orchestration, and internal developer platforms that enable teams to ship code safely and frequently.

Data sourced from Gartner DevOps research, DORA State of DevOps report, CNCF Annual Survey, and Forrester Wave for Continuous Delivery.

Market Context

Size / Scale~$22B globally; platform engineering growing 30%+ CAGR; GitOps and IaC tools fastest adoption
Growth signalPlatform engineering is the dominant trend — organizations building internal developer platforms (IDPs) to abstract infrastructure complexity and improve developer productivity
Key playersGitLab, GitHub Actions, Jenkins, CircleCI, HashiCorp, Argo, Backstage (Spotify), Humanitec, Pulumi
GeographyNorth America (40%), Europe (30%), Asia-Pacific (22%)
DORA research shows elite DevOps teams deploy 973x more frequently than low performers — yet most organizations are still in the low-to-medium performance tier. The complexity of Kubernetes, microservices, and multi-cloud deployments has created a new wave of tooling challenges.

Known Pain Points

  • CI/CD pipeline maintenance consumes 20-30% of platform engineering time — pipelines are fragile and poorly tested
  • Kubernetes complexity is overwhelming — most teams cannot effectively manage cluster security, networking, and resource optimization
  • Internal developer platforms require 6-12 months of custom development — no turnkey IDP solution exists
  • Infrastructure-as-code drift detection and policy enforcement across multi-cloud environments remains manual and error-prone

How to Choose a Niche

Internal developer platforms (IDPs), CI/CD pipeline optimization tools, and Kubernetes management platforms are the strongest niches. DevSecOps (security scanning in CI/CD) and supply chain security (SBOM, dependency scanning) are high-growth adjacent categories.
