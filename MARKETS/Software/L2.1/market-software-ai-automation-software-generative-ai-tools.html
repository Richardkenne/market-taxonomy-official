Market Context

Size / Scale~$45B globally (2024); growing 60%+ CAGR; enterprise GenAI adoption growing from 5% to 30%+ of companies in 18 months
Growth signalThe fastest-growing software category in history — driven by ChatGPT reaching 200M+ users, enterprise adoption accelerating, and AI agents emerging as the next paradigm shift
Key playersOpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Microsoft Copilot, Jasper, Writer, Glean, Perplexity, Cohere
GeographyNorth America (55%), Europe (22%), Asia-Pacific (18%)
Enterprises are experimenting with GenAI widely but struggling to move from POC to production — 75% of GenAI projects fail to reach production (Gartner). Key challenges include hallucination management, data privacy, cost unpredictability, and lack of enterprise-grade guardrails and evaluation.

Known Pain Points

  • Hallucination and factual accuracy — LLMs generate plausible but incorrect information that requires human verification for high-stakes use cases
  • Enterprise data privacy — employees paste confidential data into consumer AI tools; enterprises need private, secure AI deployments
  • Cost unpredictability — LLM API costs vary 10-100x depending on model, context length, and usage patterns; budgeting is impossible
  • AI agent reliability — autonomous AI agents fail at multi-step tasks 60%+ of the time, requiring human oversight and intervention loops

How to Choose a Niche

Vertical AI copilots (legal, healthcare, finance), enterprise AI search and knowledge management (Glean model), AI agent frameworks, and AI governance/guardrails platforms are the strongest niche opportunities. Build on top of models — do not compete with model providers.
