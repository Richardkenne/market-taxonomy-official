Marketing Automation

Marketing technology platforms automating demand generation, email campaigns, lead nurturing, account-based marketing, and attribution analytics across B2B and B2C buyer journeys.

Data sourced from Forrester Wave for B2B Marketing Automation, Gartner Magic Quadrant for Marketing Automation, and chiefmartec.com Marketing Technology Landscape.

Market Context

Size / Scale~$38B globally; B2B marketing automation growing 14% CAGR; ABM platforms fastest at 20%+
Growth signalAI-generated content, predictive lead scoring, and multi-touch attribution are reshaping the marketing stack — consolidation pressure from platforms like HubSpot
Key playersHubSpot, Marketo (Adobe), Pardot (Salesforce), Mailchimp (Intuit), ActiveCampaign, Klaviyo, 6sense, Demandbase
GeographyNorth America (48%), Europe (28%), Asia-Pacific (18%)
The average enterprise uses 91 marketing tools (chiefmartec.com) — creating data silos, attribution blindness, and an inability to connect marketing spend to revenue outcomes. The martech stack is the most bloated and fragmented area of enterprise software.

Known Pain Points

  • Multi-touch attribution remains unsolved — most companies cannot accurately attribute revenue to specific marketing activities
  • Email deliverability is declining as inbox providers tighten spam filtering — average B2B open rates below 20%
  • Lead scoring models are inaccurate — marketing-qualified leads convert to revenue at only 5-15% rates
  • ABM execution requires manual coordination between sales and marketing with fragmented data across tools

How to Choose a Niche

ABM orchestration, AI-powered content personalization, and revenue attribution platforms are the highest-growth niches. Email deliverability optimization and B2B intent data providers offer strong vertical opportunities.
