AI & Automation Software

Machine learning platforms, robotic process automation, and generative AI tools augmenting business workflows and decision-making — the fastest-growing category in enterprise software.

Segments follow Gartner’s AI and Automation taxonomy, IDC Worldwide AI Software Tracker, Forrester Automation research, and venture analysis from a16z and Sequoia — reflecting the emerging structure of the AI software market.

The global AI software market reached $90B in 2024 (IDC) and is growing at 35%+ CAGR — the fastest growth rate in enterprise software history. Generative AI alone attracted $25B+ in venture funding in 2023-2024. RPA ($14B) is maturing, while ML platforms and GenAI tools are in hypergrowth. The key question is where value accrues — model providers, infrastructure, or application-layer tools.

How to Choose a Niche

The AI software market is moving extremely fast and most categories are not yet defined. Avoid competing on model quality (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google own that layer). Instead, build AI-powered tools for specific workflows (legal document review, medical imaging analysis, code review) or AI infrastructure tools (evaluation, monitoring, fine-tuning). Vertical AI applications have the strongest defensibility.
