Poultry & Egg Production

The most efficient animal protein system, converting feed to meat at a 1.6:1 ratio versus 6:1 for beef. Global broiler production exceeds 130 million metric tons, with 1.4 trillion eggs produced annually. Highly vertically integrated — integrators like Tyson control genetics, feed, growing, processing, and distribution.

Industry data from USDA Poultry Production and Value report, International Poultry Council, and WATT Global Media poultry trends analysis.

GeographyUS (20%), China (16%), Brazil (14%), EU (13%)
Avian influenza (HPAI) has become endemic in global poultry, causing $3B+ in losses in 2022-23 alone. Biosecurity and early disease detection are no longer optional but survival requirements. Meanwhile, cage-free mandates (California Prop 12, EU ban) are forcing $5-10B in housing system conversions over the next decade.

Known Pain Points

  • HPAI biosecurity is inadequate on many farms — wild bird monitoring and rapid diagnostics remain limited
  • Cage-free transition requires massive capital investment ($40-60/bird housing cost) with uncertain consumer price acceptance
  • Broiler house environmental control (temperature, ventilation, humidity) is the #1 driver of performance yet relies on aging controller systems
  • Antibiotic-free production programs have 5-10% higher mortality with few effective alternative health management tools

How to Choose a Niche

Poultry operations are large-scale and data-driven, making them receptive to technology with proven ROI. Environmental control optimization has the broadest applicability. Biosecurity/disease surveillance is urgent but highly regulated. Cage-free transition consulting and technology is a time-limited but lucrative window.
