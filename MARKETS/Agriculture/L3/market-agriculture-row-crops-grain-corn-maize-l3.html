Precision Nitrogen Management

Variable-rate nitrogen application using sensor data, soil sampling, and weather models to optimize fertilizer use and reduce waste by 20-40% while maintaining yields.

Grain Marketing & Basis Optimization

Tools and platforms that help corn growers time sales, compare elevator bids, track basis patterns, and execute hedging strategies to capture $0.10-0.30/bushel improvements.

Post-Harvest Storage Monitoring

IoT sensors and management systems for grain bins that track temperature, moisture, and CO2 levels to prevent spoilage and optimize drying costs across on-farm storage.

How to Choose a Niche

Corn farmers adopt technology that proves per-acre ROI within one season. Nitrogen management has the highest dollar impact ($20-60/acre savings), grain marketing affects total revenue directly, and storage monitoring prevents catastrophic losses. Start with the problem where you can demonstrate measurable savings fastest.
