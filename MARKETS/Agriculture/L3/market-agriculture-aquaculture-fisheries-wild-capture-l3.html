Select a niche

Electronic Monitoring & Compliance

Camera-based catch documentation, species identification, and regulatory reporting systems mandated by an increasing number of fisheries management organizations worldwide.

Seafood Traceability & Chain of Custody

Vessel-to-plate tracking platforms that combat seafood fraud, verify sustainability certifications (MSC, ASC), and meet import requirements (EU IUU, US SIMP).

Fishing Fleet Optimization

Fuel-efficient route planning, weather routing, catch prediction, and crew management tools for commercial fishing vessels — fuel represents 30-50% of operating costs.

How to Choose a Niche

Electronic monitoring is the strongest near-term opportunity because it is increasingly mandated by regulators — compliance-driven demand is the most reliable. Traceability has large TAM but faces adoption chicken-and-egg challenges. Fleet fuel optimization has clear ROI but requires integration with existing vessel systems and navigation equipment.
