Satellite Crop Analytics & Yield Prediction

Converting free satellite imagery (Sentinel-2, Planet) into field-level crop health assessments, yield forecasts, and variable-rate prescriptions using ML-powered analytics.

Drone Crop Scouting & Mapping

UAV-based multispectral imaging for disease detection, stand count assessment, drainage mapping, and nutrient stress identification at sub-meter resolution.

Aerial Application & Drone Spraying

Commercial drone spray services for herbicide, fungicide, and biological application — already mainstream in Asian rice production and expanding to other crops and regions.

How to Choose a Niche

Satellite analytics is the most scalable business model — zero marginal cost per field once the model is trained. But imagery is commoditizing, so value comes from the prescription layer. Drone scouting is useful but hard to scale as a service business. Drone spraying has proven unit economics in Asia but faces regulatory barriers in US/EU. Focus on the analytics-to-prescription conversion.
