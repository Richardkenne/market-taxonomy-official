Sow Productivity Management

Tracking and optimizing pigs weaned per sow per year — the key profitability metric — through heat detection, farrowing management, and genetic selection tools.

Swine Health Monitoring

Early disease detection for PRRS, PED, ASF, and other pathogens using audio analysis (cough detection), environmental monitoring, and mortality pattern recognition.

Feed Optimization & Formulation

Real-time feed formulation adjustment based on ingredient prices, pig growth curves, and market weight targets to minimize cost-per-pound of gain.

How to Choose a Niche

Swine operations are data-intensive and consolidating rapidly — the top 25 US producers control 50%+ of sow inventory. African Swine Fever (ASF) has restructured global pork trade and elevated biosecurity spending. Focus on solutions that improve pigs weaned/sow/year (the industry's core KPI) or reduce feed cost per pound of gain.
