Deforestation-Free Supply Chain Traceability

Compliance platforms using satellite monitoring and blockchain to verify EU Deforestation Regulation (EUDR) requirements for soy supply chains from farm to port.

Soybean Cyst Nematode Management

Diagnostic testing, resistance screening, and integrated pest management tools for SCN — the most damaging soybean pathogen causing $1.5B+ in annual US losses alone.

Crush Margin & Biodiesel Feedstock Optimization

Analytics platforms for oilseed crushers and renewable diesel producers to optimize crush margins, feedstock sourcing, and logistics across the soy-to-fuel value chain.

How to Choose a Niche

EUDR traceability is the most urgent — mandatory compliance creates a hard deadline and willingness to pay. SCN management is a recurring farm-level pain with measurable yield impact. Crush margin optimization targets fewer but higher-value B2B customers (processors and refiners).
