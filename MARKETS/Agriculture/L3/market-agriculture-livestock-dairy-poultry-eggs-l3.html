How to Choose a Niche

Poultry integrators make decisions based on cost-per-pound of live weight. Environmental control optimization has the broadest applicability and most measurable ROI — a 1-point improvement in feed conversion saves $0.01-0.02/lb across millions of birds. Disease surveillance is urgent post-HPAI but regulatory complexity is high.
