Paddy Water Management & Methane Reduction

Sensor-based irrigation scheduling and Alternate Wetting and Drying (AWD) monitoring systems that cut water use 30% and methane emissions 48%, with carbon credit revenue potential.

Smallholder Post-Harvest Loss Prevention

Affordable drying, storage, and moisture measurement solutions for smallholder rice farmers in Southeast Asia and sub-Saharan Africa losing 15-25% of harvest value post-field.

Rice Market Price Transparency

Mobile-first price discovery and market linkage platforms for rice-growing regions where 80% of smallholders sell without access to current market pricing, losing 10-20% of potential revenue.

How to Choose a Niche

Water/methane management has the strongest institutional funding (World Bank, ADB) and carbon credit monetization. Post-harvest loss prevention is highest-impact for farmer income but requires physical product distribution. Price transparency is pure software and scalable but requires critical mass of users to provide value.
