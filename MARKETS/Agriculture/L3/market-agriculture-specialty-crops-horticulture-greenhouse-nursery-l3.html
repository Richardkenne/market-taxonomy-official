How to Choose a Niche

Greenhouse energy optimization has the broadest market and fastest payback — a 15% energy reduction on a 10-acre greenhouse saves $200K-500K annually. Vertical farm software is a smaller but fast-growing niche with high willingness to pay. Robotics in greenhouse environments benefit from the structured, controlled setting that makes automation more reliable than open-field.
