Harvest Labor Automation

Robotic and semi-automated harvesting systems for tree fruits, berries, and nuts — addressing the 40-60% labor cost challenge with vision-guided picking and shaking/catching systems.

Cold Chain Quality Monitoring

IoT sensors and analytics for temperature, humidity, and ethylene tracking from packhouse to retail shelf — preventing the 15-30% post-harvest losses in fruit supply chains.

Orchard Management & Precision Irrigation

Tree-level monitoring of water stress, nutrient status, and pest pressure in permanent crop systems using soil sensors, canopy imaging, and variable-rate irrigation.

How to Choose a Niche

Permanent crop operations (orchards, vineyards, nut groves) invest for 20+ year horizons, making them receptive to technology with multi-season payback. Labor automation has the largest TAM but longest development cycle. Cold chain monitoring and orchard precision irrigation have proven ROI and shorter sales cycles.
