Cattle Health & BRD Early Detection

Remote monitoring systems using ear tags, cameras, and AI to detect bovine respiratory disease and other illnesses 24-48 hours before visual symptoms, reducing death loss and treatment costs by $50-100/head.

Feedlot Feed Optimization & Bunk Management

Precision feeding systems that adjust rations at the pen level using intake sensors, performance data, and commodity price feeds to optimize feed conversion and reduce the 60-70% of cost that is feed.

Beef Traceability & Source Verification

Individual animal ID, blockchain-based chain of custody, and source verification platforms connecting cow-calf producers to feedlots and packers for premium program qualification and export compliance.

How to Choose a Niche

BRD early detection has the most proven ROI and growing adoption (companies like Merck Animal Health, HerdDogg are active). Feed optimization targets the largest cost center but requires integration with existing feed management systems. Traceability is regulatory-driven and increasingly mandatory for export markets.
