How to Choose a Niche

Variable-rate nitrogen is the single highest-ROI precision agriculture practice — savings of $20-60/acre are well-documented. Biologicals are growing rapidly but need better efficacy data and distribution partnerships. Fertilizer price intelligence is under-served but requires critical mass of pricing data to be valuable.
