Global EV sales hit 18M units in 2023. Fleet operators face 2030 mandates requiring electrification plans. Building electrification (gas-to-heat-pump conversions) is accelerating with IRA rebates of up to $8,000 per household. Grid capacity constraints are the #1 barrier to large-scale electrification.

Known Pain Points

  • Grid capacity and transformer limitations delaying EV charging deployments by 12-24 months
  • Total cost of ownership modeling for fleet electrification requires complex multi-variable analysis
  • Building panel upgrades required for electrification adding $5K-$20K to residential conversions
  • Workforce shortage of electricians certified for EV charger and heat pump installation

How to Choose a Niche

Fleet electrification planning software serves delivery and logistics companies with 2030 mandates. EV charging site design and utility coordination is a bottleneck service. Building electrification consulting for multifamily properties combines regulatory urgency with large contract sizes.
