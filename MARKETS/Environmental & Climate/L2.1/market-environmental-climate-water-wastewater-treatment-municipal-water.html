Market Context

Size / Scale~$120B market
Growth signalUS needs $625B in water infrastructure over 20 years (EPA); smart water growing 15%+
Key playersXylem, Veolia, Suez, Mueller Water, Badger Meter
GeographyUS (25%), Europe (25%), Asia Pacific (30%), MENA (10%)
The US has 151,000 public water systems, many with infrastructure over 50 years old. Lead pipe replacement alone requires $45B. PFAS drinking water standards finalized in 2024 will force treatment upgrades at thousands of utilities. Smart water metering adoption is at 25% in the US but growing rapidly.

Known Pain Points

  • Aging infrastructure — US water mains break every 2 minutes, losing 6 billion gallons daily
  • PFAS treatment technology costs $5M-$50M per utility with no clear reimbursement path
  • Revenue loss from non-revenue water (leaks, theft, metering errors) averaging 15-30%
  • Workforce crisis — 33% of water utility workers eligible to retire within 5 years

How to Choose a Niche

Smart water metering and leak detection is the fastest-growing technology segment. PFAS treatment for small utilities is an urgent, underserved market. Water quality monitoring IoT serves both regulatory compliance and public health.
