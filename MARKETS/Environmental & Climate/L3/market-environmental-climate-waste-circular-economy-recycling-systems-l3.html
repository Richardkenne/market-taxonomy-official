AI Robotic Waste Sorting

Computer vision and robotic arms for automated sorting at MRFs, reducing contamination and increasing material recovery rates.

Chemical Plastics Recycling

Pyrolysis, depolymerization, and solvent-based recycling converting mixed and contaminated plastics back to virgin-quality feedstock.

E-Waste Precious Metals Recovery

Extraction of gold, palladium, copper, and rare earths from circuit boards, smartphones, and electronic waste streams.

Textile Recycling Technology

Fiber-to-fiber recycling processes for cotton, polyester, and blended textiles addressing fast fashion waste.

How to Choose a Niche

AI sorting has the fastest payback period. Chemical recycling has the largest TAM. E-waste recovery has the highest margins per tonne.
