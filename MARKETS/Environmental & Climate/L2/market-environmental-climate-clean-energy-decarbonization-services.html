How to Choose a Niche

Industrial decarbonization has the largest TAM and strongest policy tailwinds (EU CBAM, IRA incentives). Energy audit software for commercial buildings offers SaaS-scale opportunity. Electrification planning for fleet operators is a near-term, high-urgency segment with 2030 mandates approaching.
