How to Choose a Niche

Smart water metering and leak detection offer SaaS-scale opportunity in municipal markets. Industrial zero-liquid-discharge is mandatory in water-stressed regions (India, Middle East). PFAS water treatment is an urgent, underserved segment. Desalination energy optimization is a high-value engineering niche.
