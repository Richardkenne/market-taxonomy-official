40 Environmental & Climate

Environmental and climate markets are segmented by problem type, technology approach, and regulatory driver — each with distinct policy dependencies, capital structures, and measurement frameworks.

We reference two types of sources: global environmental standards set by intergovernmental bodies and regulatory agencies, and market-intelligence research used to size segments and track climate investment flows. Use these to validate market definitions before narrowing to a specific environmental challenge or geography.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on segments with clear regulatory mandates or corporate compliance deadlines creating predictable demand. Examples: carbon accounting software for mid-market manufacturers facing Scope 3 reporting requirements; environmental compliance tracking for industrial operators managing multi-site permits; ESG data aggregation tools for asset managers preparing TCFD disclosures.
