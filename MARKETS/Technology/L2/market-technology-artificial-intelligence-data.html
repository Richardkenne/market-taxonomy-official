How to Choose a Niche

The AI application layer has the best risk/reward for startups — vertical AI agents for specific industries (legal, healthcare, finance) show strongest PMF signals. Data quality and governance tools are unsexy but critical infrastructure. Avoid training foundation models unless you have $100M+ in capital. Focus on domain-specific fine-tuning, evaluation frameworks, or AI-powered workflow automation where proprietary data creates defensible moats.
