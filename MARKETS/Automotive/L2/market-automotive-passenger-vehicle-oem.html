How to Choose a Niche

Look for segments where platform complexity creates procurement bottlenecks, where regulatory deadlines (emissions, safety) force rapid tooling changes, or where EV transition is disrupting legacy supply chains. SUV/crossover is highest volume; luxury offers highest margins; sports cars have the most concentrated buyer base.
