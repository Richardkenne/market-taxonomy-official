Market Context

Size / Scale~$22B market
Growth signalEnterprise drone adoption growing 15%+ annually; BVLOS waivers expanding operational scope
Key playersDJI Enterprise, Skydio, senseFly (AgEagle), Parrot ANAFI, Autel Robotics, Wingtra
GeographyNorth America (30%), Europe (25%), Asia Pacific (30%)
DJI holds 70%+ consumer and 50%+ enterprise market share despite US security restrictions. Skydio is the leading US alternative for government and defense. BVLOS (beyond visual line of sight) regulations are the key enabler — once routine, the addressable market expands 10x. Energy infrastructure inspection is the highest-value commercial use case.

Known Pain Points

  • BVLOS regulatory restrictions limiting commercial drone operations to visual line of sight
  • DJI security concerns driving costly fleet replacement for government and critical infrastructure
  • Pilot shortage and training costs for specialized inspection and mapping missions
  • Data management and integration with enterprise asset management systems

How to Choose a Niche

Drone fleet management platforms, automated inspection analytics, and BVLOS-ready autopilot systems address the highest barriers to enterprise drone adoption.
