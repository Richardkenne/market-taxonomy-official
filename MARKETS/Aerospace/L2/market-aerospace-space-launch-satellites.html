Space Launch & Satellites

Launch vehicle manufacturing and operations, satellite constellation deployment, and commercial space services — from rideshare missions to GEO communications and Earth observation platforms.

Data from Satellite Industry Association State of the Satellite Industry reports, Bryce Space and Technology, SpaceNews, and Euroconsult satellite market research.

Sources

SIA Report Bryce Space SpaceNews Euroconsult

The global satellite industry generated $281B in 2022 (SIA). Launch costs dropped 95% with reusable rockets. Over 8,000 active satellites in orbit, with mega-constellations (Starlink, OneWeb, Kuiper) driving unprecedented launch cadence — 200+ orbital launches in 2023 alone.

How to Choose a Niche

Earth observation analytics and ground segment software are the most accessible entry points. Communication satellites offer recurring revenue through bandwidth services. Small-sat launch is commoditizing rapidly — differentiate on reliability and turnaround time.
