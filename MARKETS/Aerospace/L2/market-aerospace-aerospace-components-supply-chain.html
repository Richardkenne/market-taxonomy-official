Aerospace Components & Supply Chain

Tier 1-3 suppliers of avionics, propulsion systems, aerostructures, and landing gear — with AS9100 quality standards, long qualification cycles, and critical raw material dependencies.

Data from AIA Aerospace Industries Association, PwC aerospace supply chain research, Aviation Week Network fleet data, and AS9100 certification databases.

Sources

AIA PwC A&D Aviation Week SAE AS9100

Aerospace supply chain spending exceeds $300B annually. Post-COVID recovery exposed critical bottlenecks — Airbus and Boeing both face multi-year delivery backlogs due to supplier capacity constraints. Titanium sourcing, forging capacity, and skilled labor shortages remain the top three supply chain risks.

How to Choose a Niche

Supply chain software (traceability, AS9100 compliance) is the most accessible entry point. Avionics modernization offers strong growth as legacy systems age. Raw materials present opportunities in recycling and alternative sourcing given geopolitical risks.
