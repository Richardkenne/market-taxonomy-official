AI-powered waitlist management and table optimization have the strongest growth trajectory. Reservation platforms have the largest TAM but OpenTable and Resy dominate. Guest CRM analytics is a feature, not standalone. Target high-volume restaurants where no-show prediction, dynamic party-size optimization, and automated waitlist communication directly increase covers per service and reduce lost revenue.
