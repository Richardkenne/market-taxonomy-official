Water & Wastewater Utilities

Municipal and private entities that source, treat, distribute potable water and collect, treat, and discharge wastewater in compliance with Safe Drinking Water Act and Clean Water Act standards.

Segments follow AWWA's functional breakdown of water systems and EPA's NPDES permit categories for wastewater, aligned with NACWA operational classifications.

Sources

The U.S. water and wastewater sector requires an estimated $744 billion in infrastructure investment over the next 20 years according to AWWA. PFAS regulations, lead service line replacement mandates, and aging pipe networks are creating massive capital and operational challenges for 50,000+ community water systems.

How to Choose a Niche

Prioritize segments where federal compliance deadlines are imminent. The 2024 Lead and Copper Rule Improvements and new PFAS MCLs are forcing thousands of small and mid-size water systems to upgrade treatment and replace infrastructure on compressed timelines with limited technical staff.
