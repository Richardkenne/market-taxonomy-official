How to Choose a Niche

Focus on segments where regulatory mandates create non-discretionary spending. Smart grid and AMI deployments are required by most state PUCs, while FERC Order 2222 compliance is forcing utilities to build DER management capabilities they currently lack — both create urgent, well-funded technology buying cycles.
