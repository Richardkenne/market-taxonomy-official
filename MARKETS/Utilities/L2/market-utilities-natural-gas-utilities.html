Natural Gas Utilities

Companies that produce, process, transport, store, and distribute natural gas to residential, commercial, and industrial end-users through regulated pipeline networks.

Segments follow EIA's Natural Gas value chain and FERC pipeline classifications, aligned with AGA distribution categories and PHMSA safety jurisdictions.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on segments facing the tightest regulatory pressure. Local distribution companies must comply with PHMSA pipeline replacement mandates while simultaneously managing electrification risk — creating acute demand for asset management and leak detection solutions.
