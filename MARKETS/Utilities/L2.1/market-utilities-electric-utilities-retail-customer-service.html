Customer-facing operations including billing, rate design, demand response programs, energy efficiency, and competitive retail supply in deregulated markets.

These sources track retail electricity markets, customer programs, and regulatory frameworks governing how utilities interact with end-use customers.

Market Context

Size / Scale$75B (US retail operations)
Growth signal+2-4% YoY as customer programs expand
Key playersCompetitive REPs (TXU, Direct Energy), IOU retail divisions, community choice aggregators
GeographyUS deregulated states (TX, PA, OH, IL, NY), UK, Australia, EU
Customer expectations are rising fast: utilities face pressure to offer real-time usage data, flexible rate structures, and seamless digital experiences while managing complex regulatory billing requirements.

Known Pain Points

  • Legacy CIS platforms requiring 18-24 month replacement cycles costing $50M+
  • Low customer satisfaction scores compared to other service industries
  • Demand response enrollment and dispatch across fragmented customer segments
  • Rate case preparation requiring months of manual data assembly

How to Choose a Niche

Target competitive retail electricity providers in deregulated markets (TX, PA, OH) — they face intense customer acquisition pressure and churn, creating demand for CRM, billing, and engagement tools.
