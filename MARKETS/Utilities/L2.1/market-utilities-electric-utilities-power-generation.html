Power Generation

Thermal, nuclear, hydroelectric, wind, solar, and battery generating stations producing bulk electricity for wholesale markets and vertically integrated utilities.

These sources define how power generation assets are classified, permitted, and tracked by federal regulators and industry organizations.

The generation mix is shifting rapidly: 80% of new U.S. capacity additions in 2024 were solar, wind, or battery storage, while coal retirements accelerate under EPA emissions rules.

Known Pain Points

  • Interconnection queue backlogs exceeding 5 years in most ISO/RTO regions
  • Dispatch optimization across mixed fleets of thermal, renewable, and storage assets
  • Environmental compliance tracking across multiple EPA regulations (MATS, CCR, ELG)
  • Aging thermal plant workforces with 30%+ of operators nearing retirement

How to Choose a Niche

Target independent power producers managing 500MW-5GW mixed fleets — large enough to need optimization software, small enough to lack enterprise IT teams.
