How to Choose a Niche

Radiology workflow AI is the fastest-growing niche with FDA-cleared AI triage and preliminary read tools gaining rapid adoption. Imaging center operations software has the largest TAM serving thousands of freestanding centers. Appropriate use criteria tools are mandated by CMS but commoditizing. Target radiology AI workflow tools — radiologist shortages make AI-assisted reading an urgent operational need, not a nice-to-have.
