How to Choose a Niche

Genomic result interpretation platforms have the largest growth potential as precision oncology and pharmacogenomics become standard of care. Digital pathology workflow is growing fast with whole-slide imaging adoption. Molecular test ordering is operational but lower-margin. Focus on genomic variant interpretation for oncologists — the complexity of variant databases creates a defensible moat and clear clinical value.
