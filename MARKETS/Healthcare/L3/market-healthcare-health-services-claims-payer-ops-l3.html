How to Choose a Niche

Electronic prior authorization has the hottest growth trajectory — CMS mandates API-based PA for Medicare Advantage and Medicaid by 2027. Credentialing automation has a large steady TAM with provider enrollment backlogs costing practices months of revenue. Claims intelligence is valuable but hard to sell standalone. Target electronic PA platforms where a federal mandate creates a deadline-driven buying cycle.
