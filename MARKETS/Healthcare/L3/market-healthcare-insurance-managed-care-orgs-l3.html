How to Choose a Niche

Value-based contract tools have the fastest growth as VBC adoption accelerates across commercial and government payers. ACO performance management has a large TAM with hundreds of ACOs tracking shared savings. Network optimization is important but often bundled into larger platforms. Target VBC financial reconciliation — health plans and providers both struggle to accurately model and settle value-based payments.
