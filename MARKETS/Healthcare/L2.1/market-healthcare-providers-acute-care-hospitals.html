Market Context

Size / Scale~$1.1T US hospital revenue; 6,100+ hospitals, 920,000+ staffed beds
Growth signalModerate — inpatient volume declining 1-2% annually; outpatient and observation growing
Key playersHCA Healthcare, CommonSpirit, Ascension, Kaiser Permanente, Tenet
GeographyUS-centric data; globally $5.5T+ including EU, Asia-Pacific hospital systems
Hospitals face a triple squeeze: labor shortages (nurses, techs), margin compression from payer mix shifts, and rising supply/drug costs — creating strong demand for automation, workforce optimization, and operational efficiency tools.

Known Pain Points

  • Nurse staffing shortages costing hospitals $24B+ annually in agency/travel nurse premiums
  • Clinical documentation burden consuming 2+ hours per physician per day
  • Bed management and patient flow creating ED boarding and surgical scheduling bottlenecks
  • Supply chain costs rising 15-20% since 2020 with limited procurement visibility

How to Choose a Niche

Target specific hospital operational pain points rather than broad enterprise solutions. Nurse scheduling and staffing optimization has immediate ROI. Clinical documentation and ambient AI scribes are the hottest category. Avoid competing on core clinical systems — focus on workflow gaps between EHR and operations.
