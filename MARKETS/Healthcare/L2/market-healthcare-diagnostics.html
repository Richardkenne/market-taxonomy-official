How to Choose a Niche

Clinical lab operations software (LIMS, order management) has large TAM and clear buyer. AI-powered imaging analysis is high-growth but requires FDA clearance. Digital pathology workflow tools are the sweet spot — high value, growing fast, less regulatory friction than diagnostic algorithms themselves. Avoid competing on assay development — focus on workflow and analytics layers.
