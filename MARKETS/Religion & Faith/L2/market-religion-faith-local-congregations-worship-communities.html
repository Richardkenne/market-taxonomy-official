Local Congregations & Worship Communities

Individual churches, mosques, synagogues, temples, and gurdwaras operating as the primary unit of religious gathering, worship services, pastoral care, and community stewardship.

Data from the National Congregations Study, Hartford Institute for Religion Research, Barna Group church health surveys, and ARDA congregational data.

Sources

National Congregations Study Hartford Institute Barna Group ARDA

There are approximately 380,000 congregations in the US alone (NCS Wave IV). The median congregation has 70 regular attendees, but 50% of churchgoers attend the largest 10% of congregations. Annual congregational revenue in the US exceeds $130B, with giving patterns shifting toward digital and recurring donations.

How to Choose a Niche

Congregational giving represents the largest revenue pool — digital tithing platforms for mid-size churches (200-500 attendees) are underserved. Worship production tools are growing as churches invest in hybrid in-person/online services post-COVID.
