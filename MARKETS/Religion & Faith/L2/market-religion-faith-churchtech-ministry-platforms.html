How to Choose a Niche

ChMS is the core platform with strong lock-in and switching costs. Digital giving has the clearest revenue model (transaction fees). Live-streaming tools became essential post-COVID but many churches still use consumer-grade solutions. Children's check-in is a high-compliance niche with safety requirements driving adoption.
