How to Choose a Niche

Disaster Volunteer Deployment Platforms serve organizations like Samaritan's Purse and UMCOR that mobilize thousands of volunteers per event — speed of deployment is the key differentiator. Crisis Donation Surge Management captures the 72-hour giving window when 60%+ of disaster donations occur. Long-Term Recovery Case Management is the stickiest product with 12-24 month engagement cycles per disaster.
