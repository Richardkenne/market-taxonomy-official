Missionary Support-Raising Platforms

Digital tools for individual missionaries to build donor bases, manage recurring support, send updates, and track fundraising progress toward deployment goals.

Short-Term Mission Trip Management

End-to-end platforms for organizing mission trips including team registration, fundraising, travel logistics, insurance, and in-country coordination.

Bible Translation Technology

AI-assisted translation tools, linguistic analysis software, and digital publishing platforms accelerating Bible translation into the remaining 2,000+ languages.

Global Church Partnership Networks

Platforms connecting Western churches with international partner churches for ongoing relationship, resource sharing, and collaborative ministry projects.

How to Choose a Niche

Short-Term Mission Trip Management has the highest volume — 1.6M Americans go on mission trips annually, each needing registration, fundraising, and logistics. Missionary Support-Raising Platforms replace painful letter-writing campaigns and have clear willingness to pay (3-5% of funds raised). Bible Translation Technology serves a small but deeply funded niche (Wycliffe, SIL) with complex NLP and linguistic needs.
