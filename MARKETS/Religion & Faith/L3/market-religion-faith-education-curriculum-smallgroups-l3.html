How to Choose a Niche

Video Bible Study Subscription Platforms have proven willingness to pay (RightNow Media charges $100+/month per church). VBS and Seasonal Program Management has intense but predictable demand spikes — build for the annual purchase cycle. AI-Powered Curriculum Recommendation is a differentiator but needs a content library to function, so partner with publishers first.
