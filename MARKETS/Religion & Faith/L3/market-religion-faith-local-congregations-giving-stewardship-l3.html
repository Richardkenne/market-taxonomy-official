How to Choose a Niche

Digital Tithing for Mid-Size Churches (200-1,000 attendees) is the sweet spot — large enough to pay, small enough to be underserved by enterprise tools like Pushpay. Capital Campaign Management has high willingness to pay during fundraising windows ($5K-$15K per campaign). Church Financial Transparency tools sell well post-scandal but need to be bundled with reporting to justify ongoing subscription.
