How to Choose a Niche

Children's Ministry Check-In is the highest-urgency niche — parents demand security, and churches face liability pressure. Volunteer Scheduling has high frequency of use (weekly) which drives strong retention. Church Facility Booking is a feature, not a product — avoid building it standalone unless bundled with event management.
