Congregational Civic Engagement Platforms

IRS-compliant tools for churches to facilitate voter registration, distribute nonpartisan voter guides, and mobilize congregants on policy issues.

Religious Liberty Legal Resources

Platforms providing legal guidance, case tracking, and pro-bono attorney matching for religious organizations facing regulatory or legal challenges.

Faith-Based Policy Research & Communication

Tools for faith-based policy organizations to produce research, distribute position papers, and coordinate grassroots advocacy campaigns across congregational networks.

How to Choose a Niche

Congregational Civic Engagement Platforms tap into election-cycle urgency — voter guides and advocacy toolkits sell well in 2-year cycles. Religious Liberty Legal Resources serve a well-funded niche backed by organizations like ADF and Becket Fund. Faith-Based Policy Research is grant-dependent and hard to scale — pursue institutional partnerships over direct SaaS.
