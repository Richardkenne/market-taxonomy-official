ACH & Bank Transfer Giving Solutions

Low-fee giving platforms emphasizing bank transfer (ACH) donations to reduce the 2.5% credit card processing fees that cost churches millions annually.

Generosity Analytics & Donor Intelligence

Platforms providing churches with giving trend analysis, at-risk donor identification, stewardship campaign suggestions, and year-over-year generosity benchmarking.

Recurring Giving & Subscription Tithing

Tools optimized for converting one-time givers to recurring donors through nudge campaigns, pledge management, and seamless payment method updates.

Church Capital Campaign Digital Platforms

Specialized fundraising tools for building campaigns with pledge tracking, thermometer displays, milestone celebrations, and multi-year commitment management.

How to Choose a Niche

Recurring Giving and Subscription Tithing platforms have proven unit economics with 2-3% transaction fees on predictable volume. ACH and Bank Transfer solutions win on lower fees vs card-based competitors. Generosity Analytics is a differentiator when bundled but rarely sells standalone — avoid building it as your primary product.
