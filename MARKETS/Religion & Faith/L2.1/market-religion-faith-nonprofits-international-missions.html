Market Context

Size / Scale~$15B market
Growth signalLong-term mission declining; short-term trips recovering post-COVID; Bible translation accelerating with AI
Key playersWycliffe, SIM, OMF International, Cru, IMB, YWAM
GeographySending: US (55%), South Korea (15%), UK (10%). Receiving: Africa, Asia, Latin America
The US sends 127,000+ long-term missionaries globally. Short-term mission trips involve 1.5M+ Americans annually at an average cost of $2,500-$4,000 per person. Bible translation has been completed in 724 languages with 2,000+ active translation projects.

Known Pain Points

  • Missionary support raising is manual and time-consuming — average 12-18 months to reach full funding
  • Short-term mission trip logistics (visas, insurance, in-country coordination) are complex and error-prone
  • Donor fatigue and declining per-capita missions giving among younger generations
  • Measuring mission impact beyond anecdotal stories is virtually absent

How to Choose a Niche

Missionary support-raising platforms (automating donor management and recurring giving for individual missionaries) serve a clear pain. Short-term mission trip management software combining logistics, fundraising, and team coordination is fragmented.
