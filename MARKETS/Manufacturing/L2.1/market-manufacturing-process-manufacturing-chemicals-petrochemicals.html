Chemicals & Petrochemicals

Production of basic and specialty chemicals, polymers, fertilizers, and petrochemical derivatives through continuous and batch processing at refineries and chemical plants.

Sources used to define and size this segment within the manufacturing taxonomy.

Market Context

Size / Scale~$5.7T global chemical industry output
Growth signal3-4% CAGR driven by specialty chemicals and green chemistry transition
Key playersBASF, Dow, SABIC, Sinopec, LyondellBasell, Mitsubishi Chemical
GeographyChina (40%), US (15%), Europe (15%), Middle East (10%)
Chemical plants operate 24/7 continuous processes where a single parameter deviation can cause millions in lost product, safety incidents, or environmental violations. Regulatory pressure (EPA, REACH, TSCA) is intensifying while experienced operators are retiring.

Known Pain Points

  • Process safety management requires real-time monitoring of thousands of variables across interconnected unit operations
  • Environmental compliance reporting (emissions, waste, water discharge) involves manual data aggregation from disparate systems
  • Batch-to-batch consistency in specialty chemicals demands precise recipe management that many plants still track on paper

How to Choose a Niche

Target mid-size specialty chemical producers ($50M-$500M revenue) — they face the same compliance burden as majors but with smaller IT teams and older control systems.
