The global contract manufacturing market exceeds $500B, driven by OEM asset-light strategies and supply chain diversification post-COVID. EMS alone is a $650B+ market dominated by Foxconn, Flex, and Jabil. The reshoring trend is creating new demand for North American and European contract manufacturers, though capacity constraints and workforce shortages limit growth.

How to Choose a Niche

Contract manufacturers face unique pain points around quoting accuracy, customer communication, and managing multiple concurrent programs with different specs. Small-to-mid EMS providers (under $100M revenue) lack the IT infrastructure of Foxconn/Jabil and need accessible MES, quoting, and customer portal solutions. Precision machine shops struggle with estimating, capacity planning, and managing certifications (AS9100, ISO 13485, ITAR).
