Life Insurance & Annuities

Long-duration insurance products providing death benefits, retirement income, and savings accumulation — including term life, whole life, universal life, and fixed/variable annuities.

Segments based on LIMRA/LOMA industry surveys, NAIC life insurance market data, and Swiss Re life insurance sigma reports.

Sources

Global life insurance premiums reached $2.9 trillion in 2023. The protection gap exceeds $400 trillion globally. Annuity sales hit record highs driven by rising interest rates, while digital distribution is reducing acquisition costs by 30-50% for term life products.

How to Choose a Niche

Term life with simplified underwriting is the easiest entry point — accelerated underwriting using alternative data can achieve 10x faster policy issuance. Annuity platforms and retirement planning tools have strong B2B2C distribution potential.
