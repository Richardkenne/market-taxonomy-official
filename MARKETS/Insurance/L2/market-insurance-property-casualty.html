Property & Casualty Insurance

Insurance products protecting against property damage, liability claims, and casualty losses — spanning personal auto, homeowners, commercial property, and general liability coverage.

Segments follow NAIC annual statement lines of business, AM Best market segment reports, and Swiss Re sigma world insurance studies.

Sources

NAIC Market Reports AM Best Swiss Re Sigma McKinsey Insurance

The global P&C insurance market exceeds $2.2 trillion in gross written premiums. Hard market conditions since 2020 have driven 8-12% annual rate increases. Insurtech penetration remains below 5% but is accelerating in personal lines through embedded and parametric products.

How to Choose a Niche

Personal auto is commoditized — focus on commercial lines or specialty risks where underwriting expertise creates defensible moats. Parametric insurance and embedded P&C products offer the strongest growth vectors for new entrants.
