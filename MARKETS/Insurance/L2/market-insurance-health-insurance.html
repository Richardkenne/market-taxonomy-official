Health Insurance

Global health insurance premiums exceed $2.1 trillion annually. The US accounts for 45% of global health insurance spending. Digital health integration and value-based care models are transforming payer operations, while supplemental health products grow at 12-15% annually in emerging markets.

How to Choose a Niche

Supplemental health and voluntary benefits have lower regulatory barriers than core medical. Digital health plan administration and claims processing automation offer strong SaaS opportunities. Avoid direct risk-bearing unless well-capitalized.
