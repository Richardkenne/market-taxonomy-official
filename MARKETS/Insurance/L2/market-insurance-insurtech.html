Insurance Technology (Insurtech)

Technology solutions transforming insurance value chain operations — from AI-powered underwriting and automated claims processing to digital distribution platforms and embedded insurance APIs.

Analysis based on McKinsey insurtech research, CB Insights insurance technology reports, Gallagher Re insurtech data, and Novarica insurer IT spending surveys.

How to Choose a Niche

Claims automation and embedded insurance APIs have the clearest product-market fit. Underwriting AI requires deep domain expertise but commands premium pricing. Avoid building full-stack insurers unless well-capitalized — MGA and enablement models are capital-efficient.
