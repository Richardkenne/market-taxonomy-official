Claims Automation

AI-powered claims intake, damage assessment, fraud detection, and straight-through processing platforms reducing claims cycle times and improving loss ratios.

Data from McKinsey claims transformation research, LexisNexis claims analytics, and Novarica claims technology surveys.

Market Context

Size / Scale~$18B in technology spend; 30%+ CAGR for AI claims solutions
Growth signalStraight-through processing adoption growing from 5% to 25%+ of claims
Key playersGuidewire, Duck Creek, Snapsheet, Tractable, Shift Technology, CCC Intelligent
GeographyUS (40%), Europe (30%), UK (15%), Asia Pacific (15%)
Claims processing remains the largest operational cost for insurers — automation can reduce handling costs by 30-50% while improving customer satisfaction through faster resolution.

Known Pain Points

  • First notice of loss (FNOL) still phone-based for 60%+ of claims
  • Photo/video damage assessment requires trained adjusters in most lines
  • Fraud detection rules-based systems generate excessive false positives
  • Subrogation recovery rates below optimal due to manual identification

How to Choose a Niche

Computer vision for auto and property damage assessment has clear ROI. Fraud detection using network analysis and behavioral signals can significantly improve loss ratios.
