Permanent Life Insurance

Whole life and universal life products combining lifetime death benefit coverage with cash value accumulation, investment components, and estate planning utility.

Data from LIMRA permanent life sales data, NAIC life insurance market reports, and estate planning industry research.

Sources

LIMRANAICAM BestMcKinsey

Market Context

Size / Scale~$350B in premiums globally; average policy size growing
Growth signalIUL growing at 15%+ while traditional whole life is flat; HNW segment resilient
Key playersNorthwestern Mutual, New York Life, MassMutual, Pacific Life, Lincoln Financial
GeographyUS (40%), Japan (15%), Europe (20%), Asia Pacific (15%)
Permanent life insurance remains the highest-margin life product for carriers but faces competition from alternative investment vehicles. Indexed universal life (IUL) has been the fastest-growing permanent product.

Known Pain Points

  • Product complexity makes comparison shopping nearly impossible for consumers
  • Illustration software outdated — non-guaranteed elements create confusion
  • Policy servicing and in-force management systems are legacy mainframe
  • Cash value tracking and loan management require manual processes

How to Choose a Niche

Policy illustration and comparison tools can differentiate advisors. In-force management platforms serving carriers with legacy systems have high switching costs and recurring revenue.
