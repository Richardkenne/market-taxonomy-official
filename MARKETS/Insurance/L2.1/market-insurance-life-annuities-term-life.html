Term Life Insurance

Pure protection life insurance with fixed premiums for specified periods, offering affordable death benefit coverage without cash value components.

Data from LIMRA term life sales surveys, NAIC life insurance market data, and accelerated underwriting adoption studies.

Sources

LIMRANAICSwiss ReMcKinsey

Market Context

Size / Scale~$180B globally; protection gap exceeds $25T in US alone
Growth signalAccelerated UW adoption growing from 15% to 40%+ of new policies by 2026
Key playersNorthwestern Mutual, New York Life, MassMutual, MetLife, Prudential
GeographyUS (35%), Europe (25%), Asia Pacific (25%), Rest of World
Accelerated underwriting using medical records, prescription data, and digital health assessments can reduce issuance from 6 weeks to under 48 hours for eligible applicants.

Known Pain Points

  • Protection gap: 40% of US households have no life insurance
  • Traditional underwriting takes 4-6 weeks causing application abandonment
  • Agent-dependent distribution model has high acquisition costs (150%+ of first-year premium)
  • Younger demographics perceive life insurance as unnecessary or unaffordable

How to Choose a Niche

Digital-first term life with accelerated underwriting targets the massive protection gap. Embedded life insurance at point of mortgage or loan origination has strong conversion rates.
