In-Vitro Diagnostics (IVD)

Laboratory analyzers, reagent systems, and rapid test platforms that analyze patient specimens — blood, urine, tissue — outside the body for disease detection, monitoring, and treatment guidance.

Segments follow FDA CDRH IVD product classification, IVDR (EU) risk categories, AACC clinical chemistry classifications, and EvaluateMedTech IVD market segmentation by technology platform.

Sources

The global IVD market exceeds $85B annually, accelerated post-COVID by molecular diagnostics adoption. Liquid biopsy and companion diagnostics are the fastest-growing niches at 15-20% CAGR. Software opportunities exist in laboratory information management (LIMS), AI-powered pathology, and decentralized testing data platforms.

How to Choose a Niche

LIMS modernization and lab data interoperability are the most accessible software niches. AI-powered digital pathology and genomic data interpretation platforms have high growth potential. POCT data connectivity — aggregating results from decentralized devices into EHR systems — is an underserved workflow. Avoid reagent/assay development — requires deep wet-lab capability and FDA clearance.
