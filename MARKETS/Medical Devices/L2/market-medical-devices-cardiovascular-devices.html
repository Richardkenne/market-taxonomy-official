Cardiovascular Devices

Implantable and interventional devices for treating coronary artery disease, heart rhythm disorders, structural heart conditions, and peripheral vascular disease — the largest medtech segment by revenue.

Segments follow FDA CDRH cardiovascular device panels, EvaluateMedTech cardiac device market categories, and AdvaMed cardiovascular technology classifications.

Sources

FDA CDRH EvaluateMedTech AdvaMed McKinsey MedTech

The global cardiovascular device market exceeds $60B annually. Structural heart is the fastest-growing segment at 12%+ CAGR, driven by transcatheter aortic valve replacement (TAVR) adoption and mitral valve innovation. Software opportunities exist in procedural planning, hemodynamic monitoring, and post-implant remote surveillance.

How to Choose a Niche

Software niches are most accessible — procedural planning tools using CT/echo data, remote monitoring platforms for implanted devices, and EP lab workflow optimization. Structural heart procedural planning (3D modeling from CT for TAVR sizing) is a high-value niche with clear clinical need. Avoid device manufacturing — requires Class III PMA approval and massive capital.
