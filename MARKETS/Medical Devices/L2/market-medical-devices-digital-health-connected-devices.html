Digital Health & Connected Devices

FDA-regulated wearable monitors, remote patient monitoring systems, connected drug delivery devices, and digital therapeutics platforms that generate, transmit, and act on patient health data outside traditional clinical settings.

Segments follow FDA Digital Health Center of Excellence categories, WHO Global Strategy on Digital Health classifications, MDMA connected device taxonomy, and McKinsey digital health market analysis.

How to Choose a Niche

RPM platform software is the most proven SaaS model — CMS reimbursement provides clear revenue pathway. Focus on specific chronic conditions (heart failure, diabetes, COPD) rather than building horizontal platforms. Connected device data integration middleware and clinical alert optimization are underserved. Digital therapeutics requires clinical trials — higher barrier but defensible moat if cleared.
