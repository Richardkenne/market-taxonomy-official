How to Choose a Niche

eDiscovery Processing handles the ingestion, deduplication, and normalization of electronic data for legal review — volumes are exploding with Slack, Teams, and mobile messaging. AI-powered processing that handles short-message formats and emoji/reaction context is an underserved need. The Analytics niche should focus on data volume prediction and cost estimation, because litigation budgets are set before processing begins.
