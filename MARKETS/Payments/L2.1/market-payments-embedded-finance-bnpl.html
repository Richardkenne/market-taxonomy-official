Market Context

Size / Scale~$350B transaction volume; revenue ~$25B
Growth signalConsumer BNPL maturing at 15% growth; B2B BNPL emerging at 30%+ growth
Key playersAffirm, Klarna, Afterpay (Block), PayPal, Zip, Sezzle, Billie (B2B)
GeographyUS (30%), Europe (25%), Australia (10%), Asia Pacific (20%), Rest of World
BNPL has matured from hypergrowth to sustainable economics. Regulation is increasing globally (EU, UK, Australia) but adoption continues growing. B2B BNPL (trade credit digitization) is an emerging high-value segment.

Known Pain Points

  • Consumer BNPL credit risk models underperforming at scale — loss rates rising
  • Merchant integration and checkout optimization for BNPL conversion
  • Regulatory compliance varying by jurisdiction creates operational complexity
  • Multi-lender BNPL at checkout creates consumer confusion

How to Choose a Niche

B2B BNPL for trade credit digitization has stronger unit economics than consumer. BNPL risk analytics and credit decisioning tools serve both providers and merchants.
