Real-Time & A2A Payments

Instant payment systems and account-to-account transfers including UPI, Pix, FedNow, SEPA Instant, and open banking-powered payment initiation services.

Market Context

Size / Scale~$195B in infrastructure and overlay services market
Growth signalTransaction volumes growing 40%+ globally; FedNow adoption ramping in US
Key playersNPCI (UPI), Banco Central (Pix), EBA Clearing, The Clearing House, Vocalink
GeographyIndia (45% of volume), Brazil (15%), Europe (15%), US (10%), Rest of World
Real-time payments processed 266B+ transactions in 2023. India's UPI alone handles 10B+ monthly transactions. A2A payments threaten card network revenue in markets where instant payment adoption is high.

Known Pain Points

  • Fraud prevention for irrevocable real-time payments requires pre-transaction detection
  • Request-to-pay and recurring payment mandates lack standardized overlay services
  • Merchant acceptance of A2A payments requires new checkout experiences
  • Cross-border instant payment interconnection between national systems nascent

How to Choose a Niche

Overlay services on instant payment rails (request-to-pay, recurring mandates, loyalty) have strong growth. Fraud prevention for real-time payments is a critical unmet need.
