Embedded Lending & Credit

Contextual lending products integrated at point-of-need including merchant cash advances, invoice financing, revenue-based financing, and platform-integrated credit lines for SMBs and consumers.

Data from McKinsey embedded lending research, OCC fintech lending data, and Bain SMB lending market analysis.

Market Context

Size / Scale~$45B market in platform and embedded lending infrastructure
Growth signalGrowing 25%+ as platforms embed lending; SMB embedded credit fastest-growing
Key playersStripe Capital, Square Loans, Amazon Lending, Kabbage (Amex), Shopify Capital
GeographyUS (40%), Europe (25%), Asia Pacific (20%), Rest of World
Embedded lending at point-of-sale or within business platforms achieves 3-5x higher conversion than traditional lending channels. Marketplace and platform lending models provide continuous data for underwriting.

Known Pain Points

  • Embedded credit risk models rely on platform-specific data that's hard to generalize
  • Lending compliance and state licensing requirements complex for platforms
  • Capital funding and warehouse facilities for non-bank embedded lenders
  • Collections and recovery for embedded loans lack platform-native solutions

How to Choose a Niche

Lending-as-a-service APIs enabling any platform to offer credit products without lending infrastructure have strong PMF. Revenue-based financing for SaaS and subscription businesses is a defensible niche.
