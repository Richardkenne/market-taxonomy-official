Fast Fashion & Mass Market Apparel

High-volume, trend-responsive clothing at accessible price points with rapid design-to-shelf cycles, global supply chain optimization, and omnichannel distribution from value chains to ultra-fast online retailers.

Data from Euromonitor apparel tracker, McKinsey State of Fashion, and GlobalData retail intelligence.

Sources

Euromonitor Apparel McKinsey State of Fashion Statista Apparel GlobalData Retail

Global apparel market exceeded $1.7T in 2023. Ultra-fast fashion (Shein, Temu) grew 40%+ while traditional fast fashion grew 3-5%. Overproduction remains an industry-wide problem — 30% of garments produced are never sold. Sustainability regulation (EU textile strategy) threatens the fast fashion model.
