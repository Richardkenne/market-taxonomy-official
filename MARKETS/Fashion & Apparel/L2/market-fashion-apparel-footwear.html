Footwear

Athletic, casual, formal, and specialty footwear spanning sneaker culture to work boots — driven by performance innovation, brand collaborations, resale markets, and comfort technology.

Global footwear market exceeded $380B in 2023. Athletic footwear dominates with 40%+ market share. Sneaker resale reached $30B and growing 15%+ annually. Comfort technology (Hoka, On Running) is reshaping consumer expectations across all categories. Sustainability and recycled materials are increasingly table stakes.

How to Choose a Niche

Athletic footwear has the highest innovation density and brand loyalty. Sneaker resale is a high-growth segment with authentication as a critical moat. Work and safety footwear is unglamorous but essential with recurring purchase cycles.
