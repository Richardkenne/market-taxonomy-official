Athletic & Performance Wear

Technical sportswear, athleisure, and performance apparel combining functional fabric innovation with lifestyle branding across fitness, outdoor, and casual segments.

Data from NPD/Circana sports industry tracker, Euromonitor sportswear reports, and Grand View Research.

Sources

NPD/Circana Sports Euromonitor Sportswear Statista Sportswear Grand View Research

Global sportswear market exceeded $380B in 2023. Athleisure is the fastest-growing apparel category, driven by remote work and fitness culture. Nike and Adidas control 30%+ of market share but face competition from Hoka, On Running, and Lululemon. Sustainability and recycled materials are table stakes for new brands.

How to Choose a Niche

Performance sportswear niches with strong brand loyalty and technical barriers (running shoes, compression wear) offer higher margins than commodity athleisure. Outdoor apparel has passionate communities and sustainability as a core value proposition.
