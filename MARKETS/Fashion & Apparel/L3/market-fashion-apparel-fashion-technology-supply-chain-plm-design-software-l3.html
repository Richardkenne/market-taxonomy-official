Cloud Fashion PLM

Cloud-native product lifecycle management systems for mid-market fashion brands — managing styles, materials, tech packs, and supplier collaboration without enterprise complexity.

3D Garment Visualization

3D rendering engines creating photorealistic digital garments for design review, e-commerce photography replacement, and virtual sampling across the product development cycle.

Digital Fabric Libraries

Platforms digitizing physical fabrics into accurate 3D-ready digital twins — enabling realistic virtual prototyping and reducing the need for physical swatches.

AI-Powered Pattern Making

Machine learning tools automating pattern grading, marker making, and fit optimization — reducing the manual work of traditional pattern makers while improving consistency.

How to Choose a Niche

Cloud PLM serves the mid-market gap between spreadsheets and enterprise systems. 3D visualization has proven ROI in sample cost reduction. Digital fabric libraries are the enabling technology for the entire 3D design ecosystem.
