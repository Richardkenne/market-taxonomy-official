Fashion Demand AI

Machine learning systems predicting demand for fashion products — incorporating visual attributes, trend data, weather, and social signals alongside traditional sales history.

Size & Color Optimization

Analytics tools optimizing size curve and color depth allocation at the store level — reducing the size-related stockouts and overstock that waste 10-15% of fashion inventory value.

Overstock Liquidation Platforms

B2B marketplaces and AI-powered pricing tools helping fashion brands and retailers sell excess inventory through off-price channels, flash sales, and international buyers.

Visual Assortment Planning

Visual merchandising and assortment planning tools enabling buyers to see, compare, and optimize product mixes with drag-and-drop interfaces and real-time financial projections.

How to Choose a Niche

Demand AI for new styles is the biggest unsolved problem — it's where the highest overstock waste originates. Size optimization addresses a quantifiable, widespread margin leak. Overstock liquidation serves an ongoing need across the industry.
