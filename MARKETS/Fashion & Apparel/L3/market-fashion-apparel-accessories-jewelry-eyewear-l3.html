Eyewear Virtual Try-On

Face-mapping AR technology enabling consumers to try on frames virtually — serving both DTC brands and optical retailers with accurate fit and style recommendations.

Vision Insurance Integration

Software platforms connecting eyewear retailers with vision insurance providers — automating benefit verification, claims processing, and patient eligibility checks.

Independent Optical Retail SaaS

Practice management and POS systems for independent opticians — managing prescriptions, frame inventory, lens ordering, and insurance billing.

Smart Eyewear App Ecosystem

Developer platforms and app marketplaces for smart glasses — enabling third-party applications for Meta Ray-Ban, Snap Spectacles, and enterprise AR glasses.

How to Choose a Niche

Virtual try-on is proven and scaling. Vision insurance integration serves a universal pain point for US optical retailers. Smart eyewear apps are early but could become a significant platform ecosystem.
