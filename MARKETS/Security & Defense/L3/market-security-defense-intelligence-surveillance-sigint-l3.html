How to Choose a Niche

SIGINT and Communications Intelligence is a classified, government-only market requiring Top Secret facility clearance and ITAR/EAR compliance — these barriers make it extremely defensible once you are inside. AI-powered signal processing that identifies and classifies emitters in congested electromagnetic environments is the critical capability. The Platform niche should focus on cognitive electronic warfare — systems that adapt jamming and interception strategies in real-time based on adversary behavior.
