How to Choose a Niche

Military drones and UAS is a $30B+ market accelerated by lessons from the Ukraine conflict — small, expendable tactical drones are the fastest-growing segment. AI-powered autonomous navigation and target recognition are the key technology differentiators. The Analytics niche should focus on fleet management, sortie optimization, and maintenance prediction — drone operators need to maximize uptime across hundreds of airframes.
