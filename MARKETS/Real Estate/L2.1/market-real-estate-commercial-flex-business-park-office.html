Flex / Business Park Office

Hybrid office-industrial flex spaces and serviced business parks offering flexible lease terms, shared infrastructure, and right-sized suites for SMBs and remote teams.

Data sourced from commercial real estate research firms and flex workspace analysts covering global flexible office supply, demand drivers, and operator performance.

Sources

CBRE Research JLL Research Instant Group Savills Research

Market Context

Size / Scale~$55B+ global flex office market
Growth signalFastest growing format — +15% CAGR driven by SMB demand and hybrid work adoption
Key playersIWG (Regus/Spaces), WeWork, Industrious, Convene, flex-enabled landlords
GeographyGlobal — strongest in US, UK, India, Australia; expanding in Southeast Asia and LatAm
Flex operators must manage high-frequency lease cycles, variable occupancy, and shared-service billing — operationally more like hospitality than traditional office

Known Pain Points

  • Revenue management across variable lease lengths (hourly/daily/monthly/annual)
  • Member management and community engagement at scale
  • Space utilization tracking for right-sizing and expansion decisions
  • Hybrid billing combining desk fees + amenities + overages

How to Choose a Niche

Target independent flex operators (not IWG/WeWork) managing 2-20 locations — they lack enterprise tools but have operational complexity that demands software
