Real Estate — Property Management

Day-to-day operations, tenant relations, maintenance, and financial management of residential, commercial, and industrial properties.

We reference property management industry reports, software market research, and operator surveys to define and size these segments.

Sources

NARPM JLL Research CBRE Research IFMA

Property management is one of the most fragmented and operationally intensive segments in real estate — high recurring pain, low software penetration, and strong willingness to pay for automation.

How to Choose a Niche

Residential PM has the highest fragmentation and the lowest software adoption — small landlords and mid-size operators are the best-fit buyers for automation and tenant management tools.
