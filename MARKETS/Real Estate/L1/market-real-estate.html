01 Real Estate

Real estate markets are segmented by asset type, usage, and ownership model — each with distinct operational, regulatory, and capital dynamics.

We reference two types of sources: global classification standards used by institutions and investors, and market-intelligence research used to size segments and track demand trends. Use these to validate market definitions before narrowing to a specific asset class or geography.

How to Choose a Niche

Choose a segment with a clear monetizable problem and high usage frequency. Examples: software for managing residential SMB rentals; analytics to reduce commercial landlord vacancies.
