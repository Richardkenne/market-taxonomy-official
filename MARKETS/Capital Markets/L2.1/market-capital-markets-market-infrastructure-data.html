Market data spending exceeds $38B annually and continues growing 5-8%. Alternative data adoption is mainstream with 80%+ of systematic funds and 50%+ of fundamental funds using alternative data sources.

Known Pain Points

  • Market data costs increasing 5-10% annually — budget pressure for buy-side
  • Data quality and normalization across sources inconsistent
  • Alternative data evaluation and procurement fragmented
  • Reference data management (LEI, ISIN, CUSIP) still problematic

How to Choose a Niche

Alternative data marketplace and procurement platforms serve growing demand. Market data cost optimization and usage analytics help buy-side firms manage budgets.
