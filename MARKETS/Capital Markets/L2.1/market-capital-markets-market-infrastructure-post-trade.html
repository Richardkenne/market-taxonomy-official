Trade reporting, clearing, settlement, reconciliation, regulatory compliance, and market surveillance technology for capital markets.

Market Context

Size / Scale~$22B market; RegTech growing 20%+ annually
Growth signalT+1 settlement driving modernization; regulatory reporting expanding
Key playersDTCC, Broadridge, SS&C, SmartStream, Duco, Kaizen Reporting
GeographyUS (40%), Europe (35%), Asia Pacific (20%), Rest of World
T+1 settlement in the US and pending T+1 in Europe are driving post-trade modernization. Regulatory reporting requirements (EMIR Refit, SFTR, MiFIR) continue expanding compliance technology demand.

Known Pain Points

  • T+1 settlement requires same-day trade matching and allocation
  • Regulatory reporting across multiple regimes creates duplication
  • Trade reconciliation breaks between counterparties still common
  • Market surveillance for manipulation detection increasingly complex

How to Choose a Niche

T+1 settlement readiness tools and trade matching platforms have immediate demand. Multi-regime regulatory reporting platforms reduce compliance duplication.
