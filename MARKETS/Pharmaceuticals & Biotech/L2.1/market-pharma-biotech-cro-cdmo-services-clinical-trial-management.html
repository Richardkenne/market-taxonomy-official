Clinical Trial Management (CRO)

Contract research organizations providing clinical trial design, site management, patient recruitment, data management, biostatistics, and regulatory submission support across all therapeutic areas and phases.

Data from ClinicalTrials.gov registry, IQVIA R&D outsourcing analysis, Evaluate Pharma CRO market sizing, and FDA clinical trial guidance.

ClinicalTrials.gov IQVIA Institute Evaluate Pharma FDA Clinical Trials

Market Context

Size / Scale~$65B global CRO market; growing at ~8% CAGR
Growth signalDecentralized trials accelerating; AI-driven site selection and patient matching emerging
Key playersIQVIA, Labcorp Drug Development, PPD/Thermo Fisher, Parexel, Syneos, ICON
GeographyGlobal — US, EU, and Asia-Pacific are primary clinical trial regions
The clinical CRO industry is consolidating around mega-CROs with end-to-end capabilities, but specialized boutique CROs in rare disease, oncology, and decentralized trials continue to find strong demand from emerging biotech sponsors.

Known Pain Points

  • Patient recruitment remains the #1 cause of clinical trial delays, adding months and millions in cost
  • Site selection relies on historical enrollment rather than real-time patient availability data
  • Data silos between EDC, CTMS, RTSM, and safety systems create reconciliation burden
  • Protocol amendments occurring in 60%+ of trials, each costing $500K-$1M+ in additional costs

How to Choose a Niche

AI-powered patient recruitment and matching platforms, clinical trial site performance analytics, protocol optimization tools, or decentralized trial technology platforms.
