Pharmacovigilance & Drug Safety

Systems and services for adverse event reporting, signal detection, risk management, and post-marketing safety surveillance required by FDA, EMA, and global health authorities throughout a drug product lifecycle.

Data from FDA MedWatch adverse event reporting, WHO global pharmacovigilance standards, ICH E2B(R3) safety reporting specifications, and IQVIA pharmacovigilance market data.

Sources

FDA MedWatch WHO Pharmacovigilance IQVIA Institute ICH

Market Context

Size / Scale~$8B global pharmacovigilance market; growing at ~11% CAGR
Growth signalAI case processing and signal detection; real-world data integration for safety monitoring
Key playersOracle Argus, Veeva Vault Safety, IQVIA Vigilance, ArisGlobal, Advera Health, Modus Health
GeographyGlobal — regulatory requirement in every market where drugs are sold
Pharmacovigilance case volumes are growing 15-20% annually driven by social media monitoring, patient support programs, and expanded post-marketing commitments, but 60% of case processing work remains manual.

Known Pain Points

  • Individual case safety report (ICSR) processing is 70% manual data entry and medical review
  • Signal detection in large safety databases generates excessive false positives requiring expert review
  • Aggregate reporting (PSUR/PBRER) preparation requires 6-8 weeks of manual data compilation
  • Global safety reporting timelines (15-day expedited, 90-day periodic) create constant compliance pressure

How to Choose a Niche

AI-powered ICSR intake and case processing automation, signal detection and management platforms, or real-world evidence integration for proactive safety surveillance.
