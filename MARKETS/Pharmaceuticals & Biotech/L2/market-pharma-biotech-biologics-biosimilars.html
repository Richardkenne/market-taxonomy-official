Biologics & Biosimilars

Large-molecule therapeutics derived from living systems — monoclonal antibodies, recombinant proteins, and vaccines — plus biosimilar alternatives entering post-patent markets.

Segments follow FDA CBER biologics classification, EMA biosimilar guidance, IQVIA biologics sales data, and BIO industry reports on biologic product development.

How to Choose a Niche

Biosimilar development and manufacturing is the fastest-growing opportunity. Analytical similarity testing, immunogenicity assessment, and cold chain logistics are high-value niches. For biologics, focus on manufacturing yield optimization and process intensification.
