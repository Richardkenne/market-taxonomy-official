Part A — Level 2
Cell & Gene Therapy

Advanced therapeutic modalities that modify patient cells or deliver genetic material to treat disease at its source — CAR-T, gene editing, and ex-vivo cell therapies.

Segments follow FDA CBER advanced therapy classifications, EMA ATMP designations, Alliance for Regenerative Medicine (ARM) sector reports, and NIH gene therapy clinical trial registries.

Sources

FDA Cell & Gene Therapy EMA ATMPs Alliance for Regenerative Medicine NIH/NCBI

Cell and gene therapies represent a paradigm shift toward curative medicine, with over 3,700 active clinical trials globally, but manufacturing scalability and reimbursement models for one-time treatments remain fundamental challenges.

How to Choose a Niche

Manufacturing and supply chain solutions for autologous cell therapies have the strongest near-term demand. Viral vector production is the primary bottleneck. Vein-to-vein logistics coordination and quality management software are underserved niches.
