How to Choose a Niche

The CRO/CDMO sector rewards specialization. Focus on underserved modalities (cell therapy, RNA, peptides) or underserved trial types (decentralized, adaptive, rare disease). Technology platforms that improve data flow between sponsors and CROs have strong pull.
