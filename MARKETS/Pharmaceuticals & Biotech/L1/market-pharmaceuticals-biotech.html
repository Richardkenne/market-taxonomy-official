14 Pharmaceuticals & Biotech

Pharmaceuticals and biotechnology markets are segmented by molecule type, development stage, and therapeutic modality — each with distinct regulatory pathways, patent economics, and clinical risk profiles.

We reference two types of sources: regulatory classification systems used by the FDA and EMA to categorize drug products and biologics, and life-sciences market intelligence used to size therapeutic areas and track pipeline activity. Use these to validate segment definitions before narrowing to a specific modality or indication.

01

Small Molecule Drugs

Chemically synthesized compounds targeting specific disease pathways — the traditional backbone of prescription therapeutics across cardiovascular, CNS, metabolic, and infectious disease indications.

Includes: Branded Small Molecules, Oral Solid Dosage, Controlled-Release Formulations, NCE Development.

 02

Biologics & Biosimilars

Large-molecule therapeutics derived from living systems — monoclonal antibodies, recombinant proteins, and vaccines — plus biosimilar alternatives entering post-patent markets.

Includes: Monoclonal Antibodies, Recombinant Proteins, Vaccines, Biosimilar Development.

 03

Cell & Gene Therapy

Advanced therapeutic modalities that modify patient cells or deliver genetic material to treat disease at its source — CAR-T, gene editing, and ex-vivo cell therapies.

Includes: CAR-T Cell Therapy, Gene Editing (CRISPR), Viral Vector Delivery, Ex-Vivo Cell Processing.

 04

Generic & Specialty Pharma

Off-patent drug manufacturers producing ANDA-approved generics, plus specialty pharma companies focused on niche indications, orphan drugs, and complex formulations.

Includes: Generic Drug Manufacturing, 505(b)(2) Products, Orphan Drugs, Specialty Distribution.

 05

Biotech R&D Services & CRO/CDMO

Contract research organizations running clinical trials, contract development and manufacturing organizations producing drug substance, and preclinical service providers supporting biopharma pipelines.

Includes: Clinical Trial Management (CRO), Drug Manufacturing (CDMO), Preclinical Services, Bioanalytical Labs.

 06

Pharma Technology & Commercial Operations

Software platforms for regulatory submissions, clinical data management, pharmacovigilance, medical affairs, and commercial analytics powering drug launch and lifecycle management.

Includes: eClinical Platforms, Regulatory Submission Software, Pharmacovigilance, Commercial Analytics.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on segments with heavy regulatory documentation burden and complex data workflows. Examples: electronic Trial Master File (eTMF) systems for CROs managing multi-site studies; regulatory submission software (eCTD) for biotech companies filing INDs; pharmacovigilance case management for mid-size pharma safety teams; clinical supply chain tracking for cell and gene therapy manufacturers.
