Market Context

Size / Scale~$5B market; contact data and intent signal providers growing 20%+ annually
Growth signalAI SDR tools automating outbound sequences; first-party intent signals replacing third-party data
Key playersZoomInfo, Apollo.io, Clearbit (HubSpot), Bombora, Lusha, Cognism, LeadIQ, Clay
GeographyUS (60%), UK, Germany, Canada, India, Australia
B2B contact data is commoditizing rapidly. Apollo offers near-ZoomInfo quality at a fraction of the price, and AI SDR tools (Clay, 11x, AiSDR) are automating prospecting workflows. The value is shifting from raw data access to signal quality, enrichment accuracy, and workflow integration.

Known Pain Points

  • Contact data accuracy degrades 25-30% annually as people change jobs and companies
  • Outbound email deliverability declining as inbox providers tighten anti-spam enforcement
  • Intent data providers disagree on which accounts are in-market — low correlation between vendors
  • AI SDR spam flooding inboxes, reducing response rates for all outbound practitioners

How to Choose a Niche

Build tools that improve signal quality over quantity: real-time contact data verification, multi-source intent signal triangulation, or AI-powered outbound that personalizes based on actual buying signals (not just firmographic matching). Compliance-first data platforms for GDPR markets are also underserved.
