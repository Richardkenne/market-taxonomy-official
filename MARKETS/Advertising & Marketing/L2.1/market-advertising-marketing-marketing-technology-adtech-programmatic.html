Market Context

Size / Scale~$28B in platform revenue; enabling $600B+ in programmatic ad transactions
Growth signalCTV programmatic, retail media infrastructure, and cookieless identity solutions driving growth
Key playersThe Trade Desk, Google DV360, Amazon DSP, Magnite, PubMatic, LiveRamp, Index Exchange
GeographyUS (45%), Europe, UK, Japan, Australia
The AdTech infrastructure layer is being rebuilt around privacy. Third-party cookies are deprecated, mobile identifiers are restricted, and the industry needs new identity, targeting, and measurement solutions. This creates a once-in-a-decade platform shift for startups.

Known Pain Points

  • Third-party cookie deprecation breaking audience targeting and frequency capping across the open web
  • Supply chain transparency poor — advertisers lose 15-30% of spend to intermediaries and fraud
  • CTV ad serving fragmented — no unified standard for ad insertion across streaming platforms
  • Privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA, DMA) creating compliance burden across 50+ jurisdictions

How to Choose a Niche

Build privacy-first ad infrastructure: cookieless identity graphs, contextual targeting engines, clean room technology for mid-market advertisers, or CTV ad serving standards. The market needs affordable alternatives to Google and Trade Desk for emerging channels.
