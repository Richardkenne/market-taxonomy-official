Influencer Marketing

Creator economy platforms, influencer discovery and vetting tools, campaign management, performance tracking, and creator payment infrastructure across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and emerging platforms.

Market Context

Size / Scale~$24B market; growing 25%+ annually; expected to surpass $30B by 2026
Growth signalCreator commerce, AI-powered matching, and performance-based pricing replacing flat fees
Key playersCreatorIQ, Grin, AspireIQ, Traackr, LTK, impact.com, Mavrck (Later)
GeographyUS (35%), UK, Brazil, India, Indonesia, South Korea
Influencer marketing is maturing from ad-hoc sponsorships to always-on creator programs. Brands managing 50+ creator relationships need workflow automation, contract management, and performance attribution. The rise of nano/micro-influencers (1K-50K followers) with higher engagement rates is shifting budgets from celebrity endorsements to distributed creator networks.

Known Pain Points

  • Creator fraud and fake followers remain pervasive — 15-20% of influencer audiences are bots
  • Payment and contract management manual for agencies handling 50+ creators per campaign
  • Attribution from influencer content to purchase conversion unreliable across most platforms
  • Content rights and usage licensing unclear — brands risk IP disputes when repurposing creator content

How to Choose a Niche

Build tools for scaled creator programs: automated influencer contract and payment platforms, AI-powered creator discovery matching brand values and audience demographics, or creator commerce infrastructure (affiliate tracking, creator storefronts, live shopping). Focus on the operational pain of managing 100+ creator relationships.
