27 Advertising & Marketing

Advertising & Marketing markets are segmented by channel, buying model, and service type — each with distinct measurement frameworks, agency relationships, and technology stacks.

We reference two types of sources: global classification standards used by advertising regulators and trade bodies, and market-intelligence research used to size segments and track ad-spend trends. Use these to validate market definitions before narrowing to a specific channel or geography.

How market categories are recognized How TAM is calculated
NAICS 5418 — Advertising, PR & Related Services IAB — Interactive Advertising Bureau WARC — Global Ad Spend Data Nielsen — Audience & Ad Measurement Statista Advertising & Media Outlook McKinsey — Media & Advertising

These categories follow the IAB advertising taxonomy and NAICS 5418 classification — segmenting by media channel, service function, and buyer type rather than by creative format alone.

01

Digital & Performance Advertising

Paid digital channels where advertisers buy impressions, clicks, or conversions across search, display, video, and social platforms.

Includes: Search Ads (SEM), Programmatic Display, Video Ads, Paid Social, Retargeting.

 02

Brand & Traditional Advertising

Awareness-driven advertising across television, radio, print, and out-of-home channels with broad reach and frequency-based buying.

Includes: TV & Connected TV, Radio & Audio, Print, Out-of-Home (OOH), Sponsorships.

 03

Marketing Technology (MarTech)

Software platforms for marketing automation, customer data management, attribution, and campaign orchestration across channels.

Includes: Marketing Automation, CDPs, Attribution & Analytics, CRM Marketing, Email Platforms.

 04

Creative Services & Content Marketing

Agencies, studios, and freelance networks producing brand creative, content strategy, influencer campaigns, and social media management.

Includes: Creative Agencies, Content Studios, Influencer Marketing, Social Media Management.

 05

B2B Marketing & Demand Generation

Lead generation, account-based marketing, and pipeline acceleration services targeting business buyers through intent data and outbound channels.

Includes: ABM Platforms, Intent Data Providers, Lead Generation, Sales Enablement Content.

 06

Market Research & Consumer Insights

Survey platforms, panel data providers, and analytics firms that measure consumer behavior, brand perception, and market sizing.

Includes: Survey & Panel Research, Brand Tracking, Audience Analytics, Competitive Intelligence.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on segments with measurable ROI and recurring spend cycles. Examples: automated ad-creative testing for DTC e-commerce brands; multi-touch attribution dashboards for mid-market B2B SaaS companies; influencer contract and payment management for agencies handling 50+ creator relationships.
