Brand & Traditional Advertising

Awareness-driven advertising across television, radio, out-of-home, and print channels with broad reach, frequency-based buying, and brand-building measurement frameworks.

Segments follow Magna Global media classifications, Nielsen audience measurement categories, and WARC traditional media spend breakdowns.

Sources

Traditional media still commands ~$310B in global ad spend (Magna 2024). Television remains the largest offline channel but is converging with digital through Connected TV. Out-of-home is the only traditional format growing consistently, driven by digital signage and programmatic DOOH buying.

How to Choose a Niche

Traditional media is converging with digital. Opportunities lie in the convergence layer: cross-screen measurement bridging linear TV and CTV, programmatic DOOH buying platforms for mid-market brands, and podcast ad attribution tools. Avoid pure legacy formats — focus where analog budgets are being digitized.
