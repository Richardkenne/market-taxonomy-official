How to Choose a Niche

AI is commoditizing content production but amplifying the need for quality control, brand governance, and performance measurement. Build tools that help agencies manage AI-human creative workflows, automate influencer contract and payment ops, or provide content ROI attribution. Vertical specialization (healthcare creative compliance, fintech content) commands premium pricing.
