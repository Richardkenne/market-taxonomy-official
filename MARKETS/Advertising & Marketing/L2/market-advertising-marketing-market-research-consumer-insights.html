How to Choose a Niche

Traditional research is slow and expensive. Win with speed: AI-powered qual research that synthesizes interviews in hours not weeks, real-time brand tracking dashboards for DTC brands, or competitive ad intelligence for specific verticals. The shift from periodic reports to always-on insights platforms creates recurring SaaS revenue opportunities.
