Digital Advertising

Paid digital channels where advertisers buy impressions, clicks, or conversions across search, display, video, and social platforms — representing over 65% of total global ad spend.

Segments follow IAB digital advertising classifications, GroupM global ad-spend forecasts, and eMarketer/Insider Intelligence channel breakdowns covering all major paid digital media formats.

Sources

Global digital ad spend surpassed $680B in 2024 (GroupM), with search and social commanding over 70% of budgets. The deprecation of third-party cookies and AI-driven creative optimization are reshaping how advertisers buy and measure performance across all digital channels.

How to Choose a Niche

Digital advertising is dominated by Google and Meta but the edges are wide open. Privacy-first measurement, AI creative testing, retail media network tools, and cross-channel attribution for mid-market advertisers all have strong demand. Focus on where ad budgets are shifting — CTV, retail media, and short-form video — rather than commoditized search and display buying.
