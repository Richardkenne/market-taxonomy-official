Drilling & Well Completion

The physical process of creating wellbores and preparing them for production — encompassing drilling rigs, directional drilling, hydraulic fracturing, casing, cementing, and completion hardware.

Market sizing from Rystad Energy drilling activity databases, Baker Hughes rig count data, and Spears & Associates drilling market reports tracking global rig activity and completion expenditures.

Sources

Rystad Energy Baker Hughes Rig Count Spears Research EIA Drilling Data

Market Context

Size / Scale~$150B global drilling and completion spend; ~2,300 active rigs worldwide
Growth signalInternational rig count growing 5-8% annually; US flat as efficiency gains reduce rig needs
Key playersHelmerich & Payne, Nabors, Valaris, Patterson-UTI, SLB, Halliburton, Liberty Energy
GeographyUS (Permian, Eagle Ford), Middle East, offshore Brazil, West Africa, North Sea, Australia
Drilling efficiency has improved dramatically — Permian Basin wells that took 25 days in 2014 now take 12-14 days. The next frontier is autonomous drilling, where AI-driven systems reduce non-productive time and well control incidents while addressing crew shortages on remote rigs.

Known Pain Points

  • Non-productive time (NPT) still accounts for 15-25% of drilling costs due to equipment failures and stuck pipe incidents
  • Frac sand and water logistics create bottlenecks, especially in arid basins where water sourcing is constrained
  • Well integrity failures during completion causing costly remediation and environmental liability
  • Data silos between drilling contractors, service companies, and operators preventing real-time optimization

How to Choose a Niche

Drilling is highly technical — founders with domain expertise have a significant advantage. The best software niches are real-time drilling optimization (reducing NPT), automated well planning, and frac logistics coordination. Hardware niches include downhole sensors, drilling fluid monitoring, and completion tools for unconventional reservoirs. Target the US shale market first for faster adoption cycles.
