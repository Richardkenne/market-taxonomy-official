Market Context

Size / Scale~$280B market for energy-related EPC; renewable EPC growing 20%+ annually
Growth signalStrong — energy transition driving massive capital deployment; solar/wind EPC scaling fastest
Key playersBechtel, Fluor, McDermott, Saipem, Samsung E&C, Larsen & Toubro, SunPower (solar EPC)
GeographyGlobal — Middle East (megaprojects), US (IRA pipeline), Asia-Pacific (renewables), Africa (emerging)
Energy EPC is shifting from fossil fuel megaprojects to high-volume renewable installations — requiring different project management approaches, procurement strategies, and workforce skills.

Known Pain Points

  • Solar/wind project procurement fragmented — no standardized BOM management across EPCs
  • Construction labor shortages in energy sector — 500K+ workers needed in US alone
  • Project cost overruns endemic — 80% of energy megaprojects exceed budget
  • Permitting and land acquisition processes manual and jurisdiction-specific

How to Choose a Niche

Avoid competing as an EPC contractor — instead build tools for them. Project management software for renewable EPC, procurement platforms for solar/wind components, and construction workforce management tools have strong recurring revenue.
