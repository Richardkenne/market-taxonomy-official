Refining & Processing

Converting crude oil into usable petroleum products through distillation, cracking, reforming, and treatment processes — a capital-intensive industry operating at thin margins with massive throughput volumes.

Refining margins are highly cyclical and compressed — every $0.01/gallon improvement in yield or energy efficiency translates to millions in annual savings for a large refinery. Digital optimization, advanced process control, and predictive analytics are the primary growth vectors in this mature industry.

Known Pain Points

  • Unplanned downtime costs $1-5M per day at large refineries, yet predictive maintenance adoption remains below 30%
  • Crude slate optimization is done manually or with legacy LP models that cannot adapt to real-time market shifts
  • Carbon emissions regulations (EU ETS, California LCFS) require continuous monitoring and reporting infrastructure
  • Aging refinery workforce with 40% of operators retiring within 10 years and knowledge transfer gaps

How to Choose a Niche

Refiners are notoriously conservative buyers — prove ROI in weeks, not months. Advanced process control (APC), real-time yield optimization, and predictive maintenance for critical rotating equipment have the clearest payback. Emissions monitoring and carbon accounting software is a growing compliance-driven opportunity. Target independent refiners first — they have fewer in-house digital capabilities than integrated majors.
