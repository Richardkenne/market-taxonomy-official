LNG is the fastest-growing fossil fuel segment with massive capital deployment — creating demand for project management, shipping optimization, and terminal automation solutions.

Known Pain Points

  • LNG cargo scheduling and optimization still largely manual with spreadsheet-based tracking
  • Boil-off gas management during shipping wastes 0.1-0.15% cargo per day
  • FSRU permitting and commissioning timelines unpredictable across jurisdictions
  • Gas quality interchangeability between sources creates blending challenges at terminals

How to Choose a Niche

LNG logistics optimization and cargo trading platforms are high-value niches with relatively few software competitors. Target mid-size LNG portfolio players managing 5-50 cargoes/month.
