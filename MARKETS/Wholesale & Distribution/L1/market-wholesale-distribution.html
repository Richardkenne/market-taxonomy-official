Part A — Level 1
12 Wholesale & Distribution

Wholesale & Distribution markets are segmented by product category, customer type, and service model — each with distinct margin dynamics, inventory management complexity, and technology adoption rates.

We reference two types of sources: global classification standards used by trade associations and census bureaus, and market-intelligence research used to size segments and track distribution channel evolution. Use these to validate market definitions before narrowing to a specific product vertical or geography.

01

Industrial & MRO Distribution

Distributors of industrial supplies, maintenance/repair/operations parts, safety equipment, and cutting tools serving manufacturing and facility management buyers.

Includes: Industrial Supplies, MRO Parts, Safety Equipment, Cutting Tools, Fasteners.

 02

Food & Foodservice Distribution

Broadline and specialty food distributors supplying restaurants, institutional kitchens, grocery retailers, and convenience stores with perishable and shelf-stable products.

Includes: Broadline Foodservice, Specialty Food, Produce Distribution, Frozen & Refrigerated.

 03

Medical & Healthcare Distribution

Pharmaceutical wholesalers, medical device distributors, and lab supply companies delivering regulated healthcare products to hospitals, pharmacies, and clinics.

Includes: Pharmaceutical Wholesale, Medical Devices, Lab Supplies, Dental Distribution.

 04

Technology & Electronics Distribution

IT hardware distributors, electronic component brokers, and value-added resellers connecting OEMs with enterprise buyers and channel partners.

Includes: IT Hardware, Electronic Components, Value-Added Resellers, Cloud Marketplace Distribution.

 05

Building Materials & Construction Supply

Distributors of lumber, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, and roofing materials serving general contractors, specialty trades, and residential builders.

Includes: Lumber, Plumbing & HVAC, Electrical Supply, Roofing, Concrete & Aggregates.

 06

Chemical & Specialty Distribution

Wholesale distribution of industrial chemicals, specialty chemicals, solvents, lubricants, and fluid management solutions to manufacturing, agriculture, and processing industries.

Includes: Industrial Chemicals, Specialty Chemicals, Lubricants & Fluids, Water Treatment Chemicals.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on segments with complex pricing structures, high SKU counts, or multi-location inventory challenges. Examples: dynamic pricing and rebate management tools for industrial MRO distributors; order management platforms for specialty food distributors handling cold-chain compliance; e-commerce portals for building materials suppliers enabling contractor self-service ordering.
