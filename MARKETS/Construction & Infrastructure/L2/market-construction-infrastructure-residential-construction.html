Part A — Level 2
Residential Construction

New home construction, multi-family development, and renovation projects serving the residential market — from single-family tract homes to luxury custom builds and apartment complexes.

These segments reflect the primary residential construction categories recognized by NAHB, tracked by Census Bureau housing permits, and sized by Dodge Construction Network data.

Sources

NAHB Census Bureau Housing Starts ENR McKinsey Capital Projects Dodge Construction Network

Residential construction is the largest construction segment by volume — but highly fragmented with 90% of builders producing fewer than 25 homes per year.

How to Choose a Niche

Target small-to-mid-size remodelers (5-50 jobs/year) — highest operational complexity with lowest software adoption. Estimating, scheduling, and client communication tools have strong demand.
