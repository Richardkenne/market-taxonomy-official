Fan Engagement Platforms — Niches

Select a niche within Fan Engagement Platforms to discover specific problems.

Select a niche

Fantasy Sports Platforms

Daily and season-long fantasy sports platforms where fans compete by drafting players and earning points based on real game performance.

Social Viewing & Watch Parties

Platforms enabling fans to watch sports together remotely with synchronized video, voice chat, and shared reactions.

Sports Prediction & Pick-Em Games

Gamified prediction platforms where fans make game picks, prop bets, and outcome predictions for prizes or bragging rights.

Fan Loyalty & Rewards Programs

Team and league loyalty platforms rewarding fans for attendance, merchandise purchases, content engagement, and social sharing.

How to Choose a Niche

Fantasy sports has 60M+ North American players and strong monetization through ads and entry fees. Social viewing addresses the isolation of cord-cutting — fans miss the communal experience. Prediction games operate in a lighter regulatory environment than sports betting.
