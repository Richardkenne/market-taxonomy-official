Wearable Performance Technology — Niches

Select a niche within Wearable Performance Technology to discover specific problems.

Select a niche

Athlete Load Management Platforms

Software interpreting GPS, heart rate, and accelerometer data to optimize training loads and prevent overtraining injuries.

Affordable Amateur Wearables

Low-cost performance tracking devices and apps designed for youth, amateur, and recreational athletes priced under $100.

Wearable Data Aggregation

Middleware platforms unifying data from multiple wearable brands (Garmin, Apple, Whoop, Catapult) into a single dashboard.

Biomechanical Sensor Systems

Advanced motion capture and force-plate systems measuring joint angles, ground reaction forces, and movement efficiency for injury rehabilitation.

How to Choose a Niche

Load management platforms have the highest-value use case — preventing a single injury can save teams millions. Affordable amateur wearables serve a massive underserved market. Data aggregation solves the interoperability problem that frustrates coaches using multiple device brands.
