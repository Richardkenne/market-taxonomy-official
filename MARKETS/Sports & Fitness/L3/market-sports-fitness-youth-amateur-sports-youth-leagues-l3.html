How to Choose a Niche

Youth league management software serves 100K+ leagues in the U.S. alone, most still using spreadsheets. Travel team management is a premium niche — families spend $2,500-5,000+ per season. Coach safety compliance is non-negotiable and creates sticky SaaS relationships.
