How to Choose a Niche

Regulatory compliance technology is mandatory for every sports betting operator — not optional. Real-time data feeds are the infrastructure backbone with very high switching costs. Integrity monitoring serves a regulatory mandate from leagues and governments. Responsible gambling tools are increasingly required by law.
