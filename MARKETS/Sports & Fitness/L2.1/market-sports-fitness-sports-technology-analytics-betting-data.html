U.S. legal sports betting generated $11B+ in operator revenue in 2024 following PASPA repeal in 2018. Sportradar and Genius Sports dominate the data infrastructure layer. Official league data partnerships are worth hundreds of millions annually. Responsible gambling technology is a growing regulatory requirement.

Known Pain Points

  • Regulatory compliance across 30+ U.S. states with different rules
  • Latency requirements — odds must update within milliseconds of game events
  • Match-fixing detection requiring sophisticated pattern recognition
  • Responsible gambling tools that balance user engagement with harm prevention

How to Choose a Niche

Regulatory compliance technology (geolocation, KYC, responsible gambling) is mandatory and growing. Real-time data infrastructure serves operators who need sub-second latency. Integrity monitoring platforms protect leagues from match-fixing and serve a regulatory mandate.
