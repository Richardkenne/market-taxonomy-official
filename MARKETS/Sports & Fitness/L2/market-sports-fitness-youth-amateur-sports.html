How to Choose a Niche

Youth sports management is highly fragmented — most leagues still use spreadsheets for scheduling and paper for registration. Collegiate athletics is being disrupted by NIL, creating a wave of new platform needs. Community sports organizations need affordable technology but have limited budgets, favoring freemium SaaS models.
