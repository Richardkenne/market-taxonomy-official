How to Choose a Niche

Rights fragmentation creates opportunity for aggregation and discovery tools. Niche sports lack broadcast infrastructure — streaming platforms for underserved leagues have low competition. Fan engagement is shifting from passive consumption to interactive, bet-integrated experiences.
