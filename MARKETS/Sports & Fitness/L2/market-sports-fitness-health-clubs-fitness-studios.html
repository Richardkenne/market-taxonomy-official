Health Clubs & Fitness Studios

Gym chains, boutique fitness studios, personal training operations, and membership-based wellness facilities serving consumers through recurring revenue models.

Data from IHRSA/GHFA Global Report, ClubIntel, Statista Fitness, and IBISWorld gym industry reports.

Sources

IHRSA/GHFA ClubIntel Statista Fitness IBISWorld

The global fitness industry generates over $96B annually with 200M+ members worldwide. Post-pandemic recovery has driven hybrid models combining in-person and digital fitness. Boutique studios grew 15% faster than traditional gyms, while member retention remains the industry's biggest challenge at 70% annual churn.

How to Choose a Niche

Member retention is the #1 pain point — 70% of gym members cancel within a year. Boutique studios command 3-5x higher per-visit revenue but struggle with scheduling and capacity optimization. Corporate wellness is growing fast but fragmented, creating opportunity for aggregation platforms.
