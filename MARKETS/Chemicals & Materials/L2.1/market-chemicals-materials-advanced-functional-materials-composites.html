Composites

Market Context

Size / Scale~$95B market
Growth signal7% CAGR; carbon fiber growing 12%+ for wind energy and aerospace; automated fiber placement reducing manufacturing costs
Key playersToray, Teijin, SGL Carbon, Hexcel, Solvay, Owens Corning (glass fiber)
GeographyNorth America (25%), Europe (30%), Asia Pacific (35%)
Composites are replacing metals where weight savings justify higher material costs. Wind turbine blade length growth (now 100m+) is the largest volume driver for glass and carbon fiber. Aerospace recovery post-COVID is restoring carbon fiber demand. Automotive adoption remains cost-constrained but growing for EVs.

Known Pain Points

  • Carbon fiber cost reduction from current $15-25/kg to enable automotive mass adoption
  • Composite part inspection and defect detection (delamination, porosity)
  • End-of-life composite recycling — thermoset composites are difficult to recycle
  • Automated fiber placement programming and optimization for complex geometries

How to Choose a Niche

Target composite inspection and NDT analytics, recycling technology platforms, or automated manufacturing optimization where quality assurance and cost reduction are critical adoption barriers.
