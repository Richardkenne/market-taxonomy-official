Market Context

Growth signal4% CAGR; TiO2 cyclicality; sulfuric acid demand tied to phosphate fertilizer and copper mining

GeographyChina (30%), North America (20%), Europe (15%), Africa (10% — phosphate)
Inorganic chemicals are deeply tied to mining and agriculture. Sulfuric acid is the most produced chemical globally at 260M+ tonnes, used as an indicator of industrial activity. TiO2 is highly cyclical with capacity additions driving boom-bust pricing.

Known Pain Points

  • Raw material sourcing from mining byproducts with variable quality
  • Acid plant corrosion monitoring and predictive maintenance
  • Environmental compliance for heavy metal and acid waste streams
  • TiO2 inventory management across long and volatile supply chains

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on acid plant monitoring, waste stream compliance, or mineral-to-chemical process optimization where environmental liability and process safety intersect.
