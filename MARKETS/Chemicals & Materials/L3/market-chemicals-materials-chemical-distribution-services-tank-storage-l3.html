How to Choose a Niche

Tank Farm Management System has the broadest demand — independent tank terminals manage hundreds of tanks with legacy systems, and real-time inventory visibility is a top customer requirement. Tank Inspection and Corrosion Monitor is growing as API 653 inspection intervals tighten and predictive maintenance reduces costly unplanned outages. Terminal Emission Monitoring is driven by EPA Method 21 and EU IED requirements for fugitive emission detection.
