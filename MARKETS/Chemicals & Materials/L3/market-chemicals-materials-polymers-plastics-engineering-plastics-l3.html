How to Choose a Niche

Metal-to-Plastic Conversion Feasibility has the strongest growth trajectory — lightweighting mandates in automotive and aerospace are accelerating metal replacement with engineering polymers. 3D Printing Material Qualification is the fastest-emerging niche as additive manufacturing scales beyond prototyping into production. Avoid Creep and Fatigue Life Prediction unless you have deep FEA expertise and can integrate with existing simulation tools like ANSYS.
