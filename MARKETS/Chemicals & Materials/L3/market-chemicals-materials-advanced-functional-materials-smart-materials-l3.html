How to Choose a Niche

Phase Change Material Thermal Modeler is the fastest-growing niche — data center cooling and building energy efficiency are driving massive PCM adoption. Shape Memory Alloy Design Tool serves a smaller but high-margin medical device and aerospace market. Avoid Piezoelectric Energy Harvesting Optimizer unless targeting IoT sensor manufacturers specifically.
