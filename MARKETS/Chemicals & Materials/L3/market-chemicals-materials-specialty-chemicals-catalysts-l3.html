How to Choose a Niche

Catalyst Lifecycle Management has the largest addressable market — refineries and chemical plants spend $15-20B annually on catalysts, and extending catalyst life by even 5% generates massive savings. Spent Catalyst Recovery Optimization is growing as platinum group metal prices rise and circular economy regulations tighten. Emission Catalyst Performance Monitoring is driven by tighter EPA and Euro 7 automotive emission standards.
