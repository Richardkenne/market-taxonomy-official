How to Choose a Niche

VOC Compliance Tracking is the most urgent niche — EPA and EU Directive 2004/42/EC impose strict VOC limits, and coating manufacturers face reformulation deadlines with heavy fines for non-compliance. Formulation Management Systems have the broadest demand since paint companies manage 10,000+ formulations with Excel spreadsheets. Color Matching Technology is mature and dominated by X-Rite and Datacolor — hard to enter without differentiation.
