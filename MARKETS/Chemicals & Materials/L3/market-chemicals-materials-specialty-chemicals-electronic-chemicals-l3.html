How to Choose a Niche

Ultra-High Purity Quality Systems is the highest-value niche — semiconductor fabs require parts-per-trillion purity levels and a single contamination event can destroy millions in wafers. Electronic Chemical Supply Chain Security is growing rapidly as CHIPS Act and geopolitical tensions push for domestic sourcing of critical semiconductor chemicals. CMP Slurry Optimization serves advanced node fabs but requires deep semiconductor process knowledge.
