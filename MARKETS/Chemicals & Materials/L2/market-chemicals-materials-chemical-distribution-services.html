How to Choose a Niche

Chemical distribution is ripe for digitization. Target niches in digital procurement platforms (chemical e-commerce), SDS lifecycle management, hazmat shipment compliance automation, or tank storage optimization where manual processes still dominate and compliance costs are rising.
