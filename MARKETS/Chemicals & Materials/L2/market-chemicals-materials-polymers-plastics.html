Polymers & Plastics

Thermoplastic resins, engineering plastics, and rubber compounds — including polyethylene, polypropylene, PET, and specialty elastomers for packaging, automotive, and construction.

Data from Plastics Europe market data, ICIS polymer pricing, Grand View Research plastics reports, and IHS Markit polymer analytics.

Global polymers market exceeds $650B. Packaging accounts for 40% of demand. Circular economy regulations (EU SUP Directive, EPR schemes) reshaping product design. Engineering plastics growing 6% CAGR as metal-to-plastic conversion accelerates in automotive and electronics.

Polyethylene

~$140B market

How to Choose a Niche

Polymers & plastics are volume-driven but circular economy mandates create new software needs. Target niches in recycled content tracking and certification, polymer grade selection tools, or plastics waste stream analytics where regulatory compliance creates urgency.
