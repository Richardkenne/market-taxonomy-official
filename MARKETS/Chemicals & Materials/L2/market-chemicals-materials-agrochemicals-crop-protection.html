Agrochemicals & Crop Protection

Herbicides, insecticides, fungicides, and plant growth regulators formulated for crop yield protection — with seasonal demand cycles and EPA/EFSA registration requirements.

Data from Phillips McDougall (AgbioInvestor), CropLife International, USDA pesticide data, and Grand View Research agrochemicals reports.

Sources

AgbioInvestor CropLife International EPA Pesticides Grand View Research

Global crop protection market valued at ~$75B. Herbicides dominate at 45% share. Biopesticides growing at 15%+ CAGR as integrated pest management gains adoption. Generic agrochemicals rising post-patent expiry of major active ingredients. Regulatory costs for new active ingredient registration exceed $300M.

How to Choose a Niche

Agrochemicals have extreme regulatory complexity and seasonal demand. Target niches in registration data management, resistance monitoring platforms, or precision application optimization where compliance costs and efficacy tracking create strong software demand.
