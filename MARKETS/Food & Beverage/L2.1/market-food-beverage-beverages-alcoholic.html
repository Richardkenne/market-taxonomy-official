The alcoholic beverage industry is premiumizing globally — consumers drinking less but paying more per occasion. Three-tier distribution (producer → distributor → retailer) in the US creates unique complexity and software opportunities not found in other food categories.

Known Pain Points

  • Three-tier compliance — US alcohol distribution requires navigating 50 different state regulatory frameworks for licensing, reporting, and distribution
  • Craft brewery/distillery margin pressure — small producers face 20-30% distributor margins while lacking direct sales channels
  • Inventory management across temperature zones — spirits, wine, and beer each require different storage and handling conditions
  • DTC wine/spirits shipping — state-by-state shipping laws create a patchwork of compliance requirements for online sellers

How to Choose a Niche

Alcohol compliance and distribution software is one of the best B2B SaaS niches in F&B — regulatory complexity creates high switching costs. DTC wine platforms have proven PMF. Avoid trying to disrupt three-tier distribution — build tools that make it work better.
