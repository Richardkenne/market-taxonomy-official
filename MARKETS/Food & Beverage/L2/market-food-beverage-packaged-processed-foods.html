Part A — Level 2
Packaged & Processed Foods

Shelf-stable and frozen food products including snacks, ready meals, canned goods, bakery, dairy, and confectionery sold through retail and wholesale channels worldwide.

Segments follow Euromonitor's Packaged Food taxonomy cross-referenced with USDA ERS food expenditure categories, FDA food classification codes (21 CFR), and IBISWorld food manufacturing industry reports.

Sources

Euromonitor Packaged Food USDA ERS Food Markets IBISWorld Food Mfg Mintel Food & Drink

The global packaged food market exceeds $3.5 trillion annually (Euromonitor 2024). Growth is driven by convenience demand in emerging markets, premiumization in developed economies, and clean-label reformulation across all categories. Private label penetration continues rising, squeezing branded manufacturers on margins.

How to Choose a Niche

Packaged food is highly consolidated at the brand level but fragmented at manufacturing. Software opportunities exist in production scheduling, ingredient sourcing, clean-label formulation tools, and private-label product development platforms. Avoid competing with established PLM tools — instead target mid-market manufacturers (50-500 employees) underserved by enterprise solutions.
