Functional & Specialty Foods

Health-oriented, organic, plant-based, and dietary-specific food products commanding premium pricing through ingredient innovation, wellness claims, and clean-label positioning.

Segments follow Euromonitor's Health & Wellness food taxonomy, USDA National Organic Program classifications, IFT functional food categories, and Mintel's better-for-you food trend tracking.

Sources

Euromonitor H&W Foods USDA Organic Program IFT Functional Foods Mintel Food Trends

The global health and wellness food market exceeds $1 trillion (Euromonitor 2024), growing at 6-8% CAGR — 2-3x faster than conventional packaged food. Plant-based foods have plateaued after initial hype, while probiotics, adaptogens, and personalized nutrition are accelerating. Organic food commands 20-40% price premiums but certification and supply chain verification remain complex.

How to Choose a Niche

Specialty food is the best F&B entry point for new brands — lower capital requirements and premium pricing. Software opportunities exist in organic certification management, ingredient sourcing platforms for clean-label formulations, and personalized nutrition engines. Avoid launching another plant-based meat brand — the market is oversaturated. Instead, build the picks-and-shovels infrastructure these brands need.
