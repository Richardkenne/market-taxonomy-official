Global FoodTech investment exceeded $30 billion in 2023 (AgFunder), though down from peak 2021 levels. Regulatory tailwinds are the biggest driver — FDA FSMA 204 traceability requirements and EU Farm to Fork strategy are forcing mandatory digitization. The category is shifting from consumer-facing (meal kits, grocery delivery) toward infrastructure and B2B solutions.

How to Choose a Niche

Food safety and traceability software has the strongest tailwinds — FSMA 204 creates a regulatory mandate for adoption by January 2026. Cold chain IoT is capital-light with recurring revenue. Avoid alternative protein hardware unless you have deep science expertise and patient capital. The best FoodTech businesses solve compliance and logistics problems, not moonshot science.
