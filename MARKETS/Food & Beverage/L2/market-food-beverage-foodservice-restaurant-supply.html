Foodservice & Restaurant Supply

Commercial foodservice operations including quick-service and full-service restaurants, catering, institutional dining, and the broadline and specialty distributors that supply them.

Segments follow the National Restaurant Association industry structure, Technomic foodservice segmentation, and IBISWorld restaurant industry classifications — the standard frameworks used by foodservice analysts and operators.

Sources

The global foodservice market exceeds $3.5 trillion (Euromonitor 2024). The US alone has over 1 million restaurant locations generating $1.1 trillion in sales. Ghost kitchens, third-party delivery, and labor automation are reshaping the industry faster than any period since the fast-food revolution of the 1960s.

How to Choose a Niche

Restaurant tech is one of the most active SaaS categories globally. Target multi-unit operators (5-100 locations) — they are large enough to pay but underserved by enterprise solutions. Kitchen display systems, inventory and recipe management, labor scheduling, and supplier ordering platforms all have proven PMF. Avoid general POS — that market is saturated (Toast, Square, Clover).
