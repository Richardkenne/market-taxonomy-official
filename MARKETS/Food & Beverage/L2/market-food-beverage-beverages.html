Beverages

The global beverages industry spanning alcoholic, non-alcoholic, hot drinks, and functional beverages — a $1.5 trillion market shaped by health trends, premiumization, and shifting consumption occasions.

Segments follow Euromonitor's Hot Drinks, Soft Drinks, and Alcoholic Drinks taxonomy, cross-referenced with IWSR alcohol market data and Beverage Marketing Corporation's non-alcoholic classifications.

Sources

Euromonitor Soft Drinks IWSR Drinks Market Beverage Marketing Corp. Mintel Drinks

The beverage industry is experiencing a "sober-curious" revolution: no/low-alcohol drinks grew 7% in 2023 while traditional alcohol categories stagnated. Simultaneously, functional beverages (adaptogens, nootropics, gut health) are blurring the line between drinks and supplements. Winners will master occasion-based positioning — what consumers drink depends increasingly on when and why, not just what.

How to Choose a Niche

Beverages are high-frequency purchases with strong brand loyalty but low switching costs for new occasions. Target segments where consumption patterns are shifting — the move from alcohol to functional, from sugary to clean-label, or from commodity to specialty. The best niches solve a specific occasion (energy, relaxation, hydration) better than existing options.
