Beverages

Alcoholic and non-alcoholic drink manufacturing and distribution — from carbonated soft drinks and bottled water to craft spirits, specialty coffee, and functional beverages.

Segments follow Euromonitor's Hot Drinks and Soft Drinks taxonomies, IWSR beverage alcohol classification, and USDA ERS beverage expenditure tracking — the standard frameworks used by beverage industry analysts worldwide.

Sources

Euromonitor Soft Drinks IWSR Drinks Market Analysis Euromonitor Alcoholic Drinks Rabobank Beverage Outlook

The global beverage market exceeds $2.1 trillion (Euromonitor 2024). Non-alcoholic is growing faster than alcoholic (+5% vs +2% CAGR), driven by health-conscious consumers. Functional beverages, ready-to-drink coffee, and no/low-alcohol alternatives are the fastest-growing sub-categories globally.

How to Choose a Niche

Beverage is capital-intensive for manufacturing but has strong software niches. DTC subscription platforms for craft beverages, beverage formulation tools, distributor management systems, and compliance software for alcohol regulations (TTB, state licensing) all have product-market fit. Functional beverages are the best entry point for new brands due to lower capex and premium pricing.
