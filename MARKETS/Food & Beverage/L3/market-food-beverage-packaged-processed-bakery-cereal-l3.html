Gluten-Free & Allergen-Free Bakery

Bread, pastries, and baked goods formulated without gluten, nuts, or common allergens — a $7B+ niche growing 9% CAGR, driven by celiac diagnosis rates and voluntary gluten avoidance.

High-Protein & Functional Cereals

Breakfast cereals and granola with added protein, fiber, probiotics, or adaptogens — repositioning the declining cereal category as a functional food vehicle for health-focused consumers.

Artisan & Sourdough Bread

Premium, slow-fermented bread using traditional methods — commanding 2-4x price premiums over industrial bread. Growth accelerated post-pandemic as consumers developed sourdough awareness and willingness to pay for quality.

How to Choose a Niche

Gluten-free bakery has proven demand and regulatory tailwinds (allergen labeling laws) but faces formulation challenges. Functional cereals offer a turnaround play in a declining category. Artisan bread rewards craft expertise and local distribution — think about which niche matches your operational strengths.
