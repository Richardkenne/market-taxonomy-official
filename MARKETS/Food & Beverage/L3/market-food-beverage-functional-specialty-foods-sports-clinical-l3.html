Protein & Performance Nutrition

Protein powders, pre-workouts, creatine, and recovery products — the core sports nutrition category with strong DTC channels.

Medical & Clinical Nutrition

Enteral nutrition, oral nutritional supplements, and disease-specific formulas — hospital and pharmacy distribution with clinical evidence requirements.

DNA/biomarker-based dietary recommendations, custom supplement packs, and AI-driven meal planning — emerging category at 15%+ CAGR.

How to Choose a Niche

Personalized nutrition has the most greenfield opportunity. Supplement compliance and formulation software serves the entire category. Medical nutrition has highest margins but longest sales cycles.
