Full Truckload (FTL) — Niches

Select a niche within Full Truckload (FTL) to see specific problems and begin your analysis.

Select a niche

Dry Van FTL

Standard enclosed trailer shipments for general merchandise and consumer goods.

Refrigerated (Reefer) FTL

Temperature-controlled truckload for food, pharma, and perishables requiring cold chain compliance.

Flatbed FTL

Open-deck trailers for construction materials, machinery, and oversized cargo requiring tarping and securement.

Dedicated Contract Carriage

Long-term fleet contracts where carriers dedicate trucks and drivers to a single shipper's operations.

How to Choose a Niche

Dedicated contract carriage offers the stickiest revenue and strongest margins. Reefer FTL has the highest compliance burden and technology adoption urgency.
