Logistics tech has attracted $150B+ in VC investment since 2015, yet adoption remains uneven: while large enterprises use sophisticated TMS platforms (Oracle, SAP, Blue Yonder), 65% of mid-market shippers still manage freight via email and spreadsheets. The autonomous vehicle sector has consumed $100B+ in R&D but commercial deployment remains limited to geofenced corridors, while fleet telematics has achieved 70%+ penetration in Class 8 trucks but under 30% in medium-duty fleets.

How to Choose a Niche

Fleet management for medium-duty and vocational fleets (plumbing, HVAC, delivery vans) has the lowest tech penetration and highest willingness to pay per vehicle. In freight brokerage, focus on the back-office: carrier onboarding, compliance document management, and invoice factoring are high-frequency, error-prone workflows that brokers still manage manually.
