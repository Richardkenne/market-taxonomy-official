Air Transport

Global aviation encompassing commercial passenger airlines, air cargo operations, airport infrastructure services, and business aviation — an industry that carries 4.5 billion passengers and 60+ million tonnes of freight annually.

Aviation generates $3.5T+ in global economic impact but operates on razor-thin margins (average airline net margin ~3-5%). Fuel represents 25-30% of operating costs, creating intense pressure for efficiency. The industry's post-pandemic recovery has exposed critical labor shortages in pilots, mechanics, and ground handling — driving demand for automation and workforce management solutions.

How to Choose a Niche

Focus on operational bottlenecks where legacy systems dominate — crew scheduling, MRO documentation, and cargo booking still run on decades-old platforms. Ground operations at mid-size airports remain largely manual and paper-based, creating high-value automation opportunities with lower competitive density than airline-facing solutions.
