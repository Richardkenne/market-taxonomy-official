GeographyShip management hubs in Singapore, Hong Kong, Athens, Hamburg, Mumbai
The global maritime workforce includes 1.9M seafarers, yet the industry faces a 90,000+ officer shortage (BIMCO/ICS). Crew management across 50+ flag states with different certification requirements remains one of the most complex HR challenges in any industry.

Known Pain Points

  • Crew management across multiple flag states with different certification and visa requirements
  • Planned maintenance system (PMS) compliance documentation for class surveys
  • IMO regulatory compliance tracking across SOLAS, MARPOL, MLC, and ISM Code
  • Fleet performance monitoring and fuel efficiency benchmarking across diverse vessel types

How to Choose a Niche

Ship managers handling 20-100 vessels need integrated crew management and maintenance systems but cannot afford enterprise solutions from DNV or ABS. Regulatory compliance tracking tools for the rapidly evolving IMO emissions framework (CII, EU ETS, FuelEU Maritime) are in urgent demand.
