How to Choose a Niche

Board Games & Puzzles are experiencing a renaissance driven by the kidult segment — adults buying for themselves have higher AOV and purchase year-round, not just seasonally. Action Figures & Dolls benefit from IP licensing partnerships that drive collector demand. Building Toys (bricks, construction sets) have the strongest repeat purchase behavior and longest play lifecycle.
