How to Choose a Niche

Large appliances like refrigerators and washing machines have long replacement cycles but high-ticket aftermarket services. Small kitchen appliances (air fryers, blenders) are the fastest-growing sub-niche with strong DTC potential. Cleaning appliances like robot vacuums have recurring revenue via consumables and subscription services.
