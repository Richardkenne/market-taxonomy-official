How to Choose a Niche

Digestive Health supplements have the strongest growth trajectory driven by microbiome awareness. Pain Relief is high-volume but dominated by incumbents — target the natural/alternative remedies sub-niche instead. Immunity & Vitamins have the highest consumer willingness to pay premiums and benefit from telehealth-guided OTC selection tools.
