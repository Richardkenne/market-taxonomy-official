How to Choose a Niche

Televisions & Displays is commoditized with razor-thin margins — differentiate on calibration services or smart content curation, not hardware. Headphones & Earbuds offer the best DTC margins, especially in spatial audio and ANC for remote workers. Streaming Devices are a platform play where recurring content partnerships matter more than device sales.
