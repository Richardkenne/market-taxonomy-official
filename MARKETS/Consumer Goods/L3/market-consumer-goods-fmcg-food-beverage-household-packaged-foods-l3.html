How to Choose a Niche

Snacks & Confectionery have the highest impulse purchase rates and strongest DTC subscription potential. Condiments & Sauces benefit from premiumization and artisanal trends with long shelf life reducing waste. Breakfast Cereals & Bars are shifting toward protein-rich and functional formulations where new brands can compete on ingredients rather than shelf space.
