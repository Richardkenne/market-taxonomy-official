How to Choose a Niche

Surface Cleaners dominate volume but margins are thin — differentiate via eco-friendly refill systems. Laundry Care has the strongest subscription potential with predictable usage patterns. Specialty Cleaners (oven, glass, automotive) command premium pricing and have less private-label competition than general cleaning products.
