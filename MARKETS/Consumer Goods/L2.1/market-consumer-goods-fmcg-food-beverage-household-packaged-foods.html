Part A — Level 2.1
Packaged Foods

Shelf-stable, processed, and ambient food products sold through retail and e-commerce channels globally.

These sources define how the packaged food segment is structured, sized, and tracked by CPG analysts.

Sources

Euromonitor Packaged Food Statista Food Market NielsenIQ FMCG USDA ERS Food Markets

Market Context

Size / Scale$3.2T global (Euromonitor 2024)
Growth signal+4-6% CAGR; health/wellness and plant-based segments growing faster
Key playersNestle, PepsiCo, Unilever, Kraft Heinz, Mondelez, General Mills
GeographyGlobal — North America and Europe largest; Asia-Pacific fastest growth
Private label now exceeds 25% share in mature markets. Ingredient transparency and clean-label claims drive brand differentiation.

Known Pain Points

  • SKU proliferation makes demand forecasting and shelf-space allocation complex for mid-size brands
  • Rising input costs and retailer margin pressure squeeze manufacturer profitability
  • Traceability and supply chain transparency requirements increasing across all geographies

How to Choose a Niche

Target mid-size CPG brands ($50M-$500M revenue) struggling with demand planning and retailer data integration -- they lack the scale for enterprise solutions but face the same complexity.
