Part A — Level 2.1
Traditional Toys & Games

Action figures, dolls, building sets, board games, puzzles, plush toys, and collectible trading cards.

These sources define how the traditional toys and games segment is structured and tracked.

Sources

TIA Toy Industry Data Circana Toys Tracking Euromonitor Toys Statista Toys & Hobby

Market Context

Size / Scale$45B global (Circana 2024)
Growth signal+4% CAGR; collectibles and building sets leading growth
Key playersLEGO, Mattel, Hasbro, Spin Master, MGA Entertainment, Bandai Namco
GeographyGlobal — US largest market; Europe second; China growing rapidly
Kidult (adult collectors) now account for 25% of toy industry revenue. Licensed IP from film and gaming franchises drives 35% of new product launches.

Known Pain Points

  • Extreme seasonality (40%+ of sales in Q4) creates inventory and cash flow challenges for smaller brands
  • Licensing fees for popular IP are expensive and contract terms favor licensors
  • Safety compliance testing (CPSC, EN 71, GB 6675) is costly and varies by market

How to Choose a Niche

Target the kidult segment -- adults buying toys for themselves have higher AOV, are less price-sensitive, and purchase year-round vs seasonal children's gifting.
