Pet Food & Nutrition

Dry kibble, wet food, raw/fresh diets, treats, and supplements for dogs, cats, and other companion animals.

These sources define how the pet food segment is structured and tracked by industry analysts.

APPA Pet Industry Data Euromonitor Pet Food Statista Pet Food Packaged Facts Pet Food

Market Context

Size / Scale$168B global (Euromonitor 2024)
Growth signal+7% CAGR; fresh/raw and premium tiers growing 12%+
Key playersMars Petcare (Pedigree/Royal Canin), Nestle Purina, J.M. Smucker, Hill's (Colgate), Blue Buffalo
GeographyGlobal — US largest ($60B+); Europe second; China fastest growing
Humanization drives premiumization: 45% of pet owners buy premium or super-premium food. Fresh/raw pet food (The Farmer's Dog, Ollie) is the fastest-growing format.

Known Pain Points

  • Pet food ingredient sourcing transparency lags behind human food standards despite consumer demand
  • Subscription and auto-ship models have high initial CAC but strong LTV; unit economics take 6-9 months to turn positive
  • Regulatory inconsistency between AAFCO (US) and FEDIAF (EU) complicates international expansion

How to Choose a Niche

Fresh/raw pet food delivery has proven PMF but logistics remain the bottleneck. Target the technology layer: subscription management, cold chain optimization, or personalized nutrition platforms.
