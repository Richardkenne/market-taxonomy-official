Part A — Level 2.1
Paper & Hygiene Products

Tissue, toilet paper, paper towels, diapers, feminine care, and adult incontinence products with high purchase frequency.

These sources define how the paper and hygiene segment is classified and measured.

Sources

Euromonitor Tissue & Hygiene Statista Tissue & Hygiene RISI/Fastmarkets Tissue Grand View Feminine Hygiene

Market Context

Size / Scale$180B global (Euromonitor 2024)
Growth signal+3-4% CAGR; sustainable and premium tiers growing faster
Key playersP&G, Kimberly-Clark, Essity, Unicharm, Kao, Hengan International
GeographyGlobal — Asia-Pacific largest volume; North America highest value per unit
Bamboo and recycled fiber alternatives are gaining share. Subscription models for diapers and period products have proven high retention rates.

Known Pain Points

  • Commodity pulp price volatility directly impacts margins with limited ability to pass costs to consumers
  • Sustainability pressure to reduce plastic packaging conflicts with moisture barrier requirements
  • Private label penetration is high (40%+), making brand differentiation difficult

How to Choose a Niche

Subscription replenishment for diapers and feminine care has proven product-market fit. Look for adjacent opportunities in adult incontinence -- a growing, underserved, and less price-sensitive segment.
