Consumer Electronics & Durables

Electronic devices and durable goods for personal and household use — from smartphones and laptops to home appliances and wearables — characterized by innovation cycles and planned obsolescence.

Sources

CTA — Consumer Technology Statista — Consumer Electronics IDC — Smartphone Tracker Circana — Technology

Global consumer electronics revenue reached $1.06 trillion in 2024 (CTA/Statista). AI-integrated devices, foldable form factors, and smart home convergence are driving the current upgrade cycle, while sustainability mandates push repairability requirements.
