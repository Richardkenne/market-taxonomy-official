Part A — Level 2
Toys, Games & Leisure Products

Physical and digital play products for children and adults — from action figures and board games to video game hardware and outdoor recreation equipment — heavily seasonal with 40%+ of sales in Q4.

Sources

TIA — Toy Industry Market Data Circana — Toys Tracking Statista — Toys & Hobby Euromonitor — Toys & Games

The global toy market reached $107 billion in 2024 (Circana/TIA). Kidult spending (adults buying for themselves) now accounts for 25% of toy sales. STEM/STEAM toys, collectibles, and licensed IP products from film/gaming franchises are the fastest-growing categories.
