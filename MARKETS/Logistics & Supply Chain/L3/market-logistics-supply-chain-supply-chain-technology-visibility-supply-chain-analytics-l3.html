Supply Chain Digital Twins

Virtual replicas of physical supply chains enabling real-time simulation, scenario testing, and what-if analysis for network disruption planning.

Network Optimization Platforms

Mathematical optimization tools for facility location, lane selection, mode choice, and flow allocation across distribution networks to minimize total logistics cost.

Prescriptive Analytics for Logistics

AI-driven decision engines recommending specific actions (reroute, expedite, consolidate) based on real-time supply chain conditions and constraints.

Carbon Footprint Analytics

Platforms measuring, reporting, and optimizing Scope 3 transportation emissions across supply chains with carrier-specific emission factors and reduction scenario modeling.

How to Choose a Niche

Target enterprises spending over 100 million dollars on logistics seeking network optimization. Digital twins and prescriptive analytics for network design offer the highest-value entry point.
