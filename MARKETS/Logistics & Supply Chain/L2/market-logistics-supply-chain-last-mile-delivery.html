Last-Mile Delivery

Final-leg delivery from distribution hubs to end consumers and businesses, including parcel networks, courier services, same-day platforms, and automated locker systems.

Data from Pitney Bowes Parcel Shipping Index, McKinsey last-mile research, and Last Mile Experts industry analysis.

Pitney Bowes Parcel Index McKinsey Logistics Last Mile Experts Transport Intelligence

Last-mile delivery accounts for 53% of total shipping cost. Global parcel volume exceeded 160 billion in 2023. Same-day and instant delivery growing 25%+ annually. Failed deliveries cost the industry $17B+ per year, driving demand for smart locker networks and delivery management platforms.

How to Choose a Niche

Last-mile is the most expensive and least efficient logistics segment. Route optimization for fleets with 50+ daily stops, failed-delivery reduction platforms, and locker network management for property developers offer the best technology entry points.
