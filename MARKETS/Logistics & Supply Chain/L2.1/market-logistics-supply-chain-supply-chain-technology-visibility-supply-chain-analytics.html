Supply Chain Analytics

Prescriptive and predictive analytics, digital twins, scenario planning, network optimization, and AI-driven decision support for supply chain operations.

Data from Gartner supply chain analytics research, McKinsey digital supply chain reports, and IDC supply chain intelligence forecasts.

Sources

GartnerMcKinseyIDCLLamasoft (Coupa)

Market Context

Size / Scale~$8B market
Growth signalSupply chain analytics growing 14%+; digital twin adoption accelerating post-pandemic
Key playersKinaxis, o9 Solutions, Coupa (LLamasoft), AIMMS, Optilogic
GeographyUS (35%), Europe (25%), Asia Pacific (25%)
Digital twins can model supply chain scenarios 100x faster than traditional methods. Companies with advanced analytics achieve 15-20% lower supply chain costs. Network optimization alone can reduce logistics costs 5-15% through facility location and flow optimization.

Known Pain Points

  • Data integration from ERP, TMS, WMS, and external sources for unified analytics
  • Model accuracy dependent on data quality that many organizations lack
  • Talent gap — shortage of supply chain data scientists and analytics professionals
  • Translating analytical insights into actionable operational decisions

How to Choose a Niche

Target enterprises spending $100M+ on logistics seeking network optimization and scenario planning. Digital twin and prescriptive analytics platforms for supply chain network design offer the highest-value entry point.
