How to Choose a Niche

AI-Powered Property Assessment is the most immediately valuable niche — property taxes fund 72% of local government budgets, and AI-based computer vision can assess property values from aerial imagery 10x faster than manual appraisals. Property Assessment Analytics is essential for equitable assessment — jurisdictions face lawsuits when assessment ratios show racial or geographic bias. Platform building competes with CAMA vendors like Tyler Technologies — AI-augmented valuation models are the strongest entry point.
