Problem archetypes — framework reference
01 Time / Speed
Processes too slow; delays destroy value or create friction
02 Cost / Leakage
Money wasted, margins eroded, or fees opaque
03 Coordination
Multiple parties can't sync — handoffs break down
04 Visibility / Information
Data missing, siloed, or arrives too late to act on
05 Risk / Compliance
Regulatory burden, liability exposure, or audit failures
06 Capacity / Utilization
Assets idle or over-strained — poor allocation
07 Access / Availability
Products, services, or capital hard to reach
08 Quality / Reliability
Output inconsistent, error-prone, or unpredictable
09 Trust / Verification
Identity or credentials can't be verified at scale
10 Pricing / Discovery
Prices opaque, manipulated, or impossible to benchmark
11 Incentives / Alignment
Misaligned agent interests cause bad outcomes
12 Automation / Scale
Manual workflows block growth — can't scale without headcount
